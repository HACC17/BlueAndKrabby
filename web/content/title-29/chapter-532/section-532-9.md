**§532-9 Posthumous children.** Posthumous children shall, in all cases, inherit the same as if they had been born during their father's lifetime. [CC 1859, §1456; RL 1925, §3310; RL 1935, §4818; RL 1945, §12078; RL 1955, §318-9; [HRS §532-9](/title-29/chapter-532/section-532-9/)]
