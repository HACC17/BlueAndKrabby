**§532-3 Actual possession not requisite.** It shall not be requisite that the intestate shall have been in actual possession of the property; it is sufficient if he had a good claim to it at the time of his death. [CC 1859, §1461; RL 1925, §3303; RL 1935, §4811; RL 1945, §12071; RL 1955, §318-3; [HRS §532-3](/title-29/chapter-532/section-532-3/)]
