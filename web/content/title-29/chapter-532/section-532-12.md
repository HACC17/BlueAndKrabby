**§532-12 What not advancements.** The maintaining or educating, or the giving money to a child, without a view to a portion or settlement in life, shall not be deemed an advancement. [CC 1859, §1460; RL 1925, §3313; RL 1935, §4821; RL 1945, §12081; RL 1955, §318-12; [HRS §532-12](/title-29/chapter-532/section-532-12/)]
