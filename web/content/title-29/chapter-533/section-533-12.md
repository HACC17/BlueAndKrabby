**§533-12 Barred by pecuniary provision before marriage.** Any pecuniary provision that is made for the benefit of an intended wife, and in lieu of her dower, shall, if assented to as provided in section 533-11, bar her right of dower in all the lands of her husband. [CC 1859, §1310; RL 1925, §3028; RL 1935, §4841; RL 1945, §12111; RL 1955, §319-12; [HRS §533-12](/title-29/chapter-533/section-533-12/)]
