**[ยง514E-6.5] Voting rights for time share units.** Voting rights for any apartment units designated or sold as time share units shall be as provided in rules adopted by the director of the department of commerce and consumer affairs; provided the rules shall seek to provide reasonable protection for persons who own partial interests in or rights to occupy the units.
