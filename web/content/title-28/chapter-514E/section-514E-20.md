**ยง514E-20 Effect of recording a nondisturbance agreement.** When a nondisturbance agreement has been executed by the lienholder and recorded, the lienholder, its successors, and anyone who acquires the property through foreclosure or by a deed, assignment, or other transfer in place of foreclosure, shall take the property subject to the rights of the owners.
