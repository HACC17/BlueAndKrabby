**§514A-44** **Deposit of fees.** All fees collected under this chapter shall, unless otherwise provided in this chapter, be deposited by the director of commerce and consumer affairs to the credit of the compliance resolution fund established pursuant to section 26-9(o). [L 1977, c 98, pt of §2; am L 1982, c 204, §8; am L 1983, c 124, §17; am L 1991, c 44, §14; am L 1997, c 232, §11]
