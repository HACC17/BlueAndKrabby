**[ยง523A-55] Agreement for State's payment of share of costs.** The director is authorized to enter into agreements establishing the time and manner for paying the State's proportionate share of any actual and necessary cost incurred by the United States in examining its records and reporting information to the State. Said agreements may provide for single payments at stated times over a period of years. The director shall make all payments at the time and in the manner provided in said agreements.
