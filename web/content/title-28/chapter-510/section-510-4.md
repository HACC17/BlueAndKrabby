**§510-4 Control of wife's separate property.** The wife has the same right to manage, control, dispose of, and otherwise deal with her separate property as would be applicable with respect to her property generally were it not for the enactment of this chapter. [L 1945, c 273, pt of §1; RL 1955, §326-4; [HRS §510-4](/title-28/chapter-510/section-510-4/)]
