**§501-3 Sessions.** The judge shall so arrange the sessions as to insure a prompt discharge of the business of the court. [L 1903, c 56, §4; RL 1925, §3193; RL 1935, §5002; RL 1945, §12602; RL 1955, §342-3; [HRS §501-3](/title-28/chapter-501/section-501-3/)]
