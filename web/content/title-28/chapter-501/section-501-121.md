**LEASES**

**§501-121 Leases; registration required.** Leases of registered land for a term of one year or more shall be registered. [L 1903, c 56, §64; RL 1925, §3253; RL 1935, §5063; RL 1945, §12663; am L 1951, c 142, §4; RL 1955, §342-63; [HRS §501-121](/title-28/chapter-501/section-501-121/)]

Cross References

Land subject to mortgage or lease, see §501-29.

Case Notes

Purchaser of lease noted on certificate of title holds free of unregistered sublease. 33 H. 343.

Cited: 60 H. 40, 587 P.2d 294.
