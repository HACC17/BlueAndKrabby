**§501-135 Application by trustee.** Any trustee may file an application for registration of any land held in trust by the trustee, unless expressly prohibited by the instrument creating the trust. [L 1903, c 56, §69; RL 1925, §3258; RL 1935, §5068; RL 1945, §12668; RL 1955, §342-68; [HRS §501-135](/title-28/chapter-501/section-501-135/); gen ch 1985]
