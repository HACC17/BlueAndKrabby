**§501-30 Additional facts.** The rules of court may require facts to be stated in the application in addition to those prescribed by this chapter, and not inconsistent therewith, and may require the filing of any additional papers. [L 1903, c 56, §28; RL 1925, §3217; RL 1935, §5021; RL 1945, §12621; RL 1955, §342-22; [HRS §501-30](/title-28/chapter-501/section-501-30/); am L 1972, c 91, §1(g)]
