**[§519-4]  Exemption for sustainable affordable developments.** Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, no lessee under a sustainable affordable lease as defined in section 516-1 and qualified under section 516-202 may exercise the rights granted to a lessee under section 519-2\. [L 2005, c 197, §4]
