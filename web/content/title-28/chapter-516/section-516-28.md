**§516-28 Disposition, generally.** It shall be the policy of the Hawaii housing finance and development corporation to encourage the widespread fee simple ownership of residential lots situated within a development tract. Where necessary or desirable, the corporation may lease the residential lots. Not more than one lot shall be sold in fee simple or leased to a purchaser or lessee. A husband and wife together, unless separated and living apart under a decree of separation issued by a court of competent jurisdiction, shall be entitled to only one lot.
