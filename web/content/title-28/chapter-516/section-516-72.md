**[ยง516-72]** **Civil penalty.** Failure to furnish disclosures substantially complying with the requirements of section 516-71 shall entitle the buyer to the recovery of a civil penalty of $1,000 in any proceeding at law brought within one year of the violation and the violator shall be liable further for the actual damages of the buyer, if any, reasonable attorneys' fees and court costs.
