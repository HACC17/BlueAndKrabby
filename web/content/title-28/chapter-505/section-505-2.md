**§505-2 Index of liens.** When a notice of such tax lien is recorded, the registrar shall forthwith cause the same to be indexed in the general indexes of the bureau of conveyances. [L 1931, c 220, §2; RL 1935, §5191; RL 1945, §12791; RL 1955, §346-2; am L 1966, c 33, §10; [HRS §505-2](/title-28/chapter-505/section-505-2/)]
