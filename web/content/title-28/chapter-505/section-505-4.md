**§505-4 Fees.** Unless otherwise provided by rules established by the department of land and natural resources, pursuant to chapter 91, the fees payable under this chapter are as follows:

For each notice of federal tax lien in the bureau of conveyances, $10;

For each certificate of release, partial release, or discharge of a federal tax lien in the bureau of conveyances, $10\.
