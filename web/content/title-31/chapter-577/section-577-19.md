**§577-19 Children prohibited in certain places, when; penalty.** Any keeper of a coffee shop, ice cream parlor, victualing or billiard saloon, bowling alley, skating rink, theater, show house, or premises in which any show, moving picture, or other like public entertainment is given, who permits any child under the age of sixteen years to be or remain upon the premises or in the saloon, rink, theater, show house, or premises, or in whose premises such child may be found between the hours of ten in the evening and six in the morning, unless the child is accompanied by the child's parent or guardian, or some other adult person thereto authorized by such parent or guardian, shall be fined not more than $100\.
