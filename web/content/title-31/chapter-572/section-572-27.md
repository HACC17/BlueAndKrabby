**§572-27 Persons as sureties.** All persons, upon attaining their majority, and having the necessary property qualifications as by law required, may act, serve, and be sureties on all bonds and undertakings required under the laws of the State. [L 1923, c 9, §1; RL 1925, §2997; RL 1935, §4648; RL 1945, §12369; RL 1955, §325-4; [HRS §573-4](/title-31/chapter-573/section-573-4/); ren and am L 1987, c 46, pt of §2]
