**[§577E-2] State commission on fatherhood.** There is established the state commission on fatherhood within the department of human services for administrative purposes. [L 2003, c 156, pt of §2; am L 2005, c 148, §§1, 2; ree L 2006, c 232, pt of §1; am L 2007, c 190, §1]
