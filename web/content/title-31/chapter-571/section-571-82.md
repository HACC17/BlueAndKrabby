**§571-82 Court sessions; quarters.** Sessions of the court shall be held at such places as the court shall determine, subject to section 603-14\. [L 1965, c 232, pt of §1; Supp, §333-37; [HRS §571-82](/title-31/chapter-571/section-571-82/); am L 1973, c 211, §1(j)]
