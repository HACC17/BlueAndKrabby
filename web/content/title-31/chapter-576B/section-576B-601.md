**ARTICLE 6\. REGISTRATION, ENFORCEMENT, AND**

**MODIFICATION OF SUPPORT ORDER**

Note

Article heading amended by L 2015, c 77, pt of §1.

**PART I. REGISTRATION FOR ENFORCEMENT OF SUPPORT ORDER**

Note

Part heading amended by L 2015, c 77, pt of §1.

**§576B-601** **Registration of order for enforcement.** A support order or income withholding order issued in another state or a foreign support order may be registered in this State for enforcement. [L 1997, c 295, pt of §1; am L 2015, c 77, pt of §1]
