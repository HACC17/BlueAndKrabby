**ยง576B-506 Contest by obligor.** (a) An obligor may contest the validity or enforcement of an income withholding order issued in another state and received directly by an employer in this State by registering the order in a tribunal of this State and filing a contest to that order as provided in Article 6, or otherwise contesting the order in the same manner as if the order had been issued by a tribunal of this State.

(b) The obligor shall give notice of the contest to:

(1) A support enforcement agency providing services to the obligee;

(2) Each employer that has directly received an income withholding order relating to the obligor; and

(3) The person designated to receive payments in the income withholding order or, if no person is designated, to the obligee. [L 1997, c 295, pt of ยง1; am L 2015, c 77, pt of ยง1]
