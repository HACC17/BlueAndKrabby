**ยง576B-603** **Effect of registration for enforcement.** (a) A support order or income withholding order issued in another state or a foreign support order is registered when the order is filed in the family court of this State.

(b) A registered support order issued in another state or a foreign country is enforceable in the same manner and is subject to the same procedures as an order issued by a tribunal of this State.

(c) Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, a tribunal of this State shall recognize and enforce, but may not modify, a registered support order if the issuing tribunal had jurisdiction. [L 1997, c 295, pt of ยง1; am L 2015, c 77, pt of ยง1]
