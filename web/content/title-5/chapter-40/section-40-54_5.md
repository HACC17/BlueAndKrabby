**[ยง40-54.5]** **Disclosure of information.** (a) The appropriate government agencies shall disclose to the recipient of payroll deductions information related to the administration of payroll deductions as follows: name, social security number, and amounts and dates of both voluntary and mandatory payroll deductions remitted to the recipient.

(b) An employee organization or other recipient receiving government records pursuant to this section shall be subject to the same restrictions on disclosure of the records as the originating agency. [L 1990, c 250, ยง3]
