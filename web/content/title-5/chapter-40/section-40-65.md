**§40-65 Notice of payment of warrant notes.** When there are sufficient moneys in the treasury to pay warrant notes, the director of finance shall give public notice for one week stating therein that the warrant notes whose numbers appear in the notice are payable, and that interest shall cease upon all the called warrant notes ten days after the first notice.
