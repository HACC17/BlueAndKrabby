**§40-94 Examination under oath.** The comptroller shall examine upon oath, which oath the comptroller may administer, all persons whom the comptroller thinks fit to examine touching the receipts and expenditures of money, and touching all other things and matters necessary for the due execution of the powers vested in the comptroller by this chapter.
