**ยง38-7 Responsibility of director.** The director shall not be responsible for any moneys deposited in a depository under this chapter, but the State through its director shall be chargeable with the safekeeping of the bonds deposited with the director as security for deposits of state money, and of the proceeds of any sale of the bonds made under this chapter.
