**[ยง266-2.2]** **Exemption from conservation district permitting and site plan approval requirements**. Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, all work involving submerged lands used for state commercial harbor purposes shall be exempt from any permitting and site plan approval requirements established for lands in a conservation district.
