**[ยง269-40]** **Access to advanced services.** The commission shall ensure that all consumers are provided with nondiscriminatory, reasonable, and equitable access to high quality telecommunications network facilities and capabilities that provide subscribers with sufficient network capacity to access information services that provide a combination of voice, data, image, and video, and that are available at just, reasonable, and nondiscriminatory rates that are based on reasonably identifiable costs of providing the services.
