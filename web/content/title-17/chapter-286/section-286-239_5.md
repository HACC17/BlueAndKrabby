**ยง286-239.5 Reactivation of expired commercial driver's license; fees; road test waived.** (a) Unless revoked or suspended, and except as provided in subsection (b), any commercial driver's license that has expired under section 286-239 or rules adopted pursuant to section 286-246 may be reactivated by the licensee in accordance with the requirements and procedures set forth for the renewal of commercial drivers' licenses under section 286-239(g). No person seeking reactivation of an expired commercial driver's license under this subsection shall be required to undergo reexamination of the person's driving skills under section 286-236\. The examiner of drivers shall require the holder of an expired commercial driver's license to pay a reactivation fee of $5 for each thirty-day period, or fraction thereof, that has elapsed after a ninety-day grace period.

(b) Any commercial driver's license not reactivated under subsection (a) within one year of the indicated date of expiration shall be invalid. The examiner of drivers shall examine an applicant whose commercial driver's license has been declared invalid under this subsection in accordance with the licensing procedures established under sections 286-236 and 286-239\.
