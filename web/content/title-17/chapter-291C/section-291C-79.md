**[ยง291C-79] Pedestrians' right-of-way on sidewalks.** The driver of a vehicle emerging from or entering an alley, building, private road, or driveway shall yield the right-of-way to any pedestrian approaching on any sidewalk extending across the alley, building entrance, road, or driveway.
