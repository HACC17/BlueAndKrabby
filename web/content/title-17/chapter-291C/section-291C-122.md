**[ยง291C-122] Limitations on backing.** (a) The driver of a vehicle shall not back it unless such movement can be made with safety and without interfering with other traffic.

(b) The driver of a vehicle shall not back it upon any shoulder or roadway of any controlled-access highway. [L 1971, c 150, pt of ยง1]
