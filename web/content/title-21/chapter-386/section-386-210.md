**ยง386-210 Cease and desist orders.** (a) After notice and opportunity for a hearing, the insurance commissioner may issue an order requiring a person or group to cease and desist from engaging in an act or practice found to be in violation of any provision of this chapter or any rule adopted under this chapter.

(b) Upon a finding, after notice and opportunity for a hearing, that any person or group has violated any cease and desist order, the insurance commissioner may do either or both of the following:

(1) Impose a monetary penalty of not more than $10,000 for each and every act or violation of the order, but not to exceed an aggregate monetary penalty of $100,000; or

(2) Revoke the group's certificate of approval or any insurance license held by the person. [L 1986, c 304, pt of ยง1]
