**ยง392-63 Disbursements from the fund.** Expenditures of moneys in the trust fund for disability benefits shall not be subject to any provisions of law requiring specific appropriations or other formal release by state officers of money in their custody. All benefits shall be paid from the fund upon warrants drawn upon the director of finance by the comptroller of the State supported by vouchers approved by the director.
