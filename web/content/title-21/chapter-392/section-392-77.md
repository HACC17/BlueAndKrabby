**[ยง392-77] Payment of benefits.** Benefits shall be paid promptly in accordance with the decision. If an application for reconsideration is duly made or if judicial review is duly filed, benefits with respect to weeks of disability not in dispute and benefits payable in any amount not in dispute shall be paid promptly regardless of any reconsideration or appeal.
