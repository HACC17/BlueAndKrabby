**[****ยง398A-5] Employee rights.** (a) An employer shall not interfere with, restrain, or deny the exercise of, or an attempt to exercise, a right established by this chapter.

(b) An employer shall not discharge, fine, suspend, expel, discipline, or in any other manner discriminate against an employee who:

(1) Exercises a right provided under this chapter; or

(2) Opposes a practice made unlawful by this chapter. [L 2014, c 161, pt of ยง1]
