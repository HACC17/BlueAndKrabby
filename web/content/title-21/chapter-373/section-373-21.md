**[ยง373-21] Exemption of vocational rehabilitation services.** Providers of vocational rehabilitation services for injured workers covered by approved worker compensation plans under section 386-25 shall not be considered employment agencies under this section; provided that the providers confine their services to those specified under section 386-25 and do not hold themselves out to the public as employment agencies under this chapter.
