**§605-5 License fee.** The clerk of the supreme court shall exact from every practitioner, upon the practitioner's receiving a license, an admission fee of $10, for the benefit of the State. [CC 1859, §1073; RL 1925, §2308; RL 1935, §3607; RL 1945, §9705; RL 1955, §217-5; [HRS §605-5](/title-32/chapter-605/section-605-5/); gen ch 1985]
