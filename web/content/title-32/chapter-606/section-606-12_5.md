**[§606-12.5]  Certified shorthand reporter; administration of oaths or affirmations.** A certified shorthand reporter may administer oaths or affirmations and may perform the duties of a deposition officer pursuant to rules of court relating to persons before whom depositions may be taken. [L 2010, c 110, §1]
