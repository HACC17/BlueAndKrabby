**ยง601-3.5** **Supreme court law library revolving fund.** There is established a revolving fund for the statewide supreme court law library system into which shall be deposited all fines, fees, and other revenue derived from the system's operations. Moneys deposited in this fund may be expended to replace or repair lost, damaged, stolen, unreturned, or outdated books, serials, periodicals, and other library materials, or to support and improve library services.
