**§601-9 Same, other instances.** No attorney shall be employed, or allowed to appear before any court, in any action or proceeding which has been previously tried before the attorney as a judge. [CC 1859, pt of §821; RL 1925, §2214; RL 1935, §3574; RL 1945, §9575; RL 1955, §213-5; [HRS §601-9](/title-32/chapter-601/section-601-9/); am L 1972, c 88, §1(d); gen ch 1985]
