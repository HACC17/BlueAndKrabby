**§601-15 Style of process.** The style of process in the state courts shall run in the name of "The State of Hawaii", and all prosecutions shall be carried on in the name and by the authority of the State of Hawaii. [L Sp 1959 1st, c 5, §7; Supp, §213-12; [HRS §601-15](/title-32/chapter-601/section-601-15/); am L 1972, c 88, §1(g)]
