**§604-15 Rules by supreme court.** The supreme court may from time to time, by general rules, prescribe forms for carrying section 604-13 into effect, and make all other needful regulations. [L 1868, p 21; RL 1925, §2282; RL 1935, §3772; RL 1945, §9682; RL 1955, §216-13; [HRS §604-15](/title-32/chapter-604/section-604-15/)]
