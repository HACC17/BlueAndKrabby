**§607-23 Fees of commissioners, appraisers, etc.** Fees of commissioners and appraisers shall be such as the court deems just and reasonable, together with actual and necessary traveling expenses at the same rate paid for traveling expenses to witnesses subpoenaed, and all actual disbursements for surveying, plans, etc. [RL 1935, pt of §3793; am L 1941, c 293, §1; RL 1945, §9761; am L 1945, c 37, §2; RL 1955, §219-22; am L 1963, c 85, §3; [HRS §607-23](/title-32/chapter-607/section-607-23/); am L 1972, c 88, §5(v)]
