**§602-7 Oaths, subpoenas.** The supreme court may compel the attendance of witnesses and the production of books, papers, documents or tangible things, and any justice may administer oaths. [L 1892, c 57, §52; RL 1925, §2226; RL 1935, §3595; RL 1945, §9606; RL 1955, §214-6; [HRS §602-7](/title-32/chapter-602/section-602-7/); am L 1972, c 88, §2(c); ree L 1979, c 111, pt of §2]
