**§602-9 Sessions, where.** The supreme court shall sit in Honolulu; provided that the chief justice may appoint a different place for the sitting of the court, pro tempore. [L 1892, c 57, §55; am L 1901, c 22, §2; RL 1925, §2228; RL 1935, §3597; RL 1945, §9608; RL 1955, §214-8; [HRS §602-9](/title-32/chapter-602/section-602-9/); am L 1972, c 88, §2(e); ree L 1979, c 111, pt of §2]
