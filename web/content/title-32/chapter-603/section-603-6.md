**§603-6 Residence of judge.** Every judge of a circuit court shall reside in the circuit for which the judge is appointed. [L 1892, c 57, pt of §30; RL 1925, §2242; RL 1935, §3638; RL 1945, §9636; RL 1955, §215-6; [HRS §603-6](/title-32/chapter-603/section-603-6/); gen ch 1985]
