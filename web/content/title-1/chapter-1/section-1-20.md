**§1**-**20 "Month", "year".** The word "month" means a calendar month; and the word "year", a calendar year. [CC 1859, §18; RL 1925, §17; RL 1935, §18; RL 1945, §17; RL 1955, §1-25; [HRS §1-20](/title-1/chapter-1/section-1-20/)]
