**[ยง6F-4] Organization; quorum; meetings.** The board shall elect from its own membership, a chairperson and a vice-chairperson. The board shall meet at least four times a year and a majority of all members to which the board is entitled shall constitute a quorum to do business. The board shall adopt such rules as are necessary in carrying out the purposes of this chapter. [L 1990, c 211, pt of ยง3]
