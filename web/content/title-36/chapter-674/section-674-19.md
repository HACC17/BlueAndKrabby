**§674-19 Limitation on actions.** Every claim cognizable under this part shall forever be barred unless the action is commenced by December 31, 1999\. [L 1991, c 323, pt of §1; am L 1993, c 351, §12; am L Sp 1995, c 14, §15; am L 1997, c 382, §7]
