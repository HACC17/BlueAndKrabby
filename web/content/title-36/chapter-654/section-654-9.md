**[§654-9] Other remedies not affected.** The remedy provided by this chapter shall be in addition to, and shall not be deemed to affect, any other remedy. [L Sp 1949, c 7, pt of §1; RL 1955, §244-29; [HRS §654-30](/title-36/chapter-654/section-654-30/); am L 1972, c 90, §1(g); ren [HRS §654-9](/title-36/chapter-654/section-654-9/)]
