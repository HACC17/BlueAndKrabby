**§663-13 Judgment against one tortfeasor.** The recovery of a judgment by the injured person against one joint tortfeasor does not discharge the other joint tortfeasors. [L 1941, c 24, §3; RL 1945, §10489; RL 1955, §246-12; [HRS §663-13](/title-36/chapter-663/section-663-13/)]
