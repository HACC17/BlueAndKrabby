**[ยง663-8.3] Loss or impairment of earning capacity; damages.** (a) In all tort cases where damages are awarded for loss or impairment of earning capacity, the amount of probable future earnings shall be determined by taking into account the effect of probable taxes.

(b) Nothing in this section shall be construed to limit or restrict the use of other factors deemed appropriate by a court in calculating damages awarded for loss or impairment of earning capacity. [L Sp 1986, c 2, ยง18]
