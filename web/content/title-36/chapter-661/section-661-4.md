**§661-4 Action by alien, when.** Aliens who are citizens or subjects of a government that accords to citizens of this State the right to prosecute claims against that government in its courts shall have the privilege of prosecuting claims against the State under this part. [L 1894-5, c 26, §4; RL 1925, §2672; RL 1935, §4423; RL 1945, §10478; RL 1955, §245-4; [HRS §661-4](/title-36/chapter-661/section-661-4/); am L 1972, c 164, §1(d); am L 2016, c 55, §43]
