**[§659-7] Judgment according to nature of complaint.** In all cases contemplated by sections 659-1 to 659-9, judgment shall be given according to the nature of the complaint made. [L 1876, c 39, §42; RL 1925, §2722; RL 1935, §4264; RL 1945, §10285; RL 1955, §236-25; [HRS §659-52](/title-36/chapter-659/section-659-52/); am L 1972, c 90, §3(g); ren [HRS §659-7](/title-36/chapter-659/section-659-7/)]
