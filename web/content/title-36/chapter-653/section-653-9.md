**§653-9 Service on beneficiary.** Service of process upon the beneficiary may be made as has been usual or shall be provided in the case of civil actions in general. [L 1890, c 50, §7; RL 1925, §2852; RL 1935, §4298; RL 1945, §10329; RL 1955, §238-9; [HRS §653-9](/title-36/chapter-653/section-653-9/)]
