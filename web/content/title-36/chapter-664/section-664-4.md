**§664-4 Copy sent to department.** Each commissioner of boundaries shall, within thirty days after issuing a certificate of boundaries, deposit a certified copy thereof in the office of the department of land and natural resources, and shall also deposit a certified copy of the approved or adopted plan thereof with the department.
