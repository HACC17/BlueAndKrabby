**[ยง657D-5] Extension of benefits to persons ordered to report for state military service.** Any person who is ordered to report for state military service shall be entitled to the relief and benefits during:

(1) The period of actual military service; and

(2) The period beginning on the date of receipt of the order and ending on the date upon which the member reports for military service, or the date on which the order is revoked, whichever is earlier. [L 1994, c 257, pt of ยง2]
