**§657-15 Two or more disabilities.** Where there are two or more such disabilities existing at the time the right of action accrued, the limitations herein prescribed shall not attach until all the disabilities are removed. [CC 1859, §1047; RL 1925, §2650; RL 1935, §3921; RL 1945, §10432; RL 1955, §241-14; [HRS §657-15](/title-36/chapter-657/section-657-15/)]
