**§657-36 Same.** If, when the right of action first accrues, the person entitled thereto is under any of the disabilities mentioned in section 657-34, and dies without having recovered the premises, no further time for making the entry or bringing the action, beyond what is prescribed in section 657-35, shall be allowed by reason of the disability of any other person. [L 1870, c 22, §6; RL 1925, §2662; RL 1935, §3933; RL 1945, §10444; RL 1955, §241-35; [HRS §657-36](/title-36/chapter-657/section-657-36/)]
