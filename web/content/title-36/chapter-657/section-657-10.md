**§657-10 Special limitations.** This part shall not extend to any action which is, or shall be, limited by any statute to be brought within a shorter time than is herein prescribed; but the action shall be brought within the time limited by the statute. [CC 1859, §1048; RL 1925, §2647; RL 1935, §3918; RL 1945, §10429; RL 1955, §241-9; [HRS §657-10](/title-36/chapter-657/section-657-10/)]
