**§657-2 Mutual current account.** In all actions in the nature of debt, account, or assumpsit, brought to recover any balance due upon a mutual, open, and current account, the cause of action shall be deemed to have accrued from the time of the last item proved in the account. [CC 1859, §1038; RL 1925, §2640; RL 1935, §3911; RL 1945, §10422; RL 1955, §241-2; [HRS §657-2](/title-36/chapter-657/section-657-2/); am L 1972, c 105, §1(b)]
