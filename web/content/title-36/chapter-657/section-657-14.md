**§657-14 Disability to exist at accrual of action.** No person shall avail oneself of any disability enumerated in this part, unless the disability existed at the time the right of action accrued. [CC 1859, §1046; RL 1925, §2649; RL 1935, §3920; RL 1945, §10431; RL 1955, §241-13; [HRS §657-14](/title-36/chapter-657/section-657-14/); am L 1972, c 105, §1(g); gen ch 1985]
