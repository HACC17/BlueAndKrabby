**§662-9 Costs.** In an action under this chapter, court costs and fees as set by law may be allowed to the prevailing party. [L 1957, c 312, pt of §1; Supp, §245A-9; [HRS §662-9](/title-36/chapter-662/section-662-9/); am L 1972, c 164, §2(c); am L 1979, c 152, §3]

Case Notes

This section and §662-12 not inconsistent as to attorney's fees. 51 H. 540, 465 P.2d 580 (1970).
