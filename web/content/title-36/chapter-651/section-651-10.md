**§651-10 Indemnity for police officer.** If the police officer has any reasonable doubt as to the ownership of the property, or as to its liability to be taken on the writ, the police officer may require sufficient security to indemnify the police officer for attaching it. [L 1905, c 84, §10; RL 1925, §2814; RL 1935, §4199; RL 1945, §10150; RL 1955, §233-10; [HRS §651-10](/title-36/chapter-651/section-651-10/); gen ch 1985]
