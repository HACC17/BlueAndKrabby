**§651-19 Recording discharge.** Whenever an order has been made discharging or releasing an attachment upon real property, a certified copy of the order may be recorded in the office in which a copy of the writ has been recorded under section 651-9 and be indexed in like manner. All expenses relating to the attachment shall be paid by the plaintiff. [L 1905, c 84, §19; RL 1925, §2823; RL 1935, §4208; RL 1945, §10159; RL 1955, §233-19; am L 1966, c 33, §8; [HRS §651-19](/title-36/chapter-651/section-651-19/)]

Case Notes

Section not applicable where an order discharging or releasing an attachment upon real property has not been made. 3 H. App. 89, 641 P.2d 989 (1982).
