**§651-11 Execution in order of receipt.** When there are several attachments against the same defendant, they shall be executed in the order in which they are received by the police officer. [L 1905, c 84, §11; RL 1925, §2815; RL 1935, §4200; RL 1945, §10151; RL 1955, §233-11; [HRS §651-11](/title-36/chapter-651/section-651-11/)]
