**ยง667-39 Right to enforce this part.** (a) The foreclosing mortgagee, any other creditor having a recorded lien on the mortgaged property before the recordation of the notice of default and intention to foreclose under section 667-23, the borrower, and the mortgagor, may enforce this part by bringing an action in the circuit court of the circuit where the mortgaged property is located.
