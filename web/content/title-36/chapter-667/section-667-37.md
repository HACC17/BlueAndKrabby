**§667-37 Judicial action of foreclosure before public sale.** This part shall not prohibit the foreclosing mortgagee, or any other creditor having a recorded lien on the mortgaged property before the recordation of the notice of default under section 667-23, from filing an action for the judicial foreclosure of the mortgaged property in the circuit court of the circuit where the mortgaged property is located; provided that the action is filed before the public sale is held. [L 1998, c 122, pt of §1; am L 2011, c 48, §31; am L 2012, c 182, §24; am L 2013, c 197, §2]
