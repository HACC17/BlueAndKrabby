**§660-29 Discharge, when.** If no legal cause for the imprisonment or restraint is shown the person shall be immediately discharged therefrom. [L 1870, c 32, §21; RL 1925, §2752; RL 1935, §4337; RL 1945, §10378; RL 1955, §239-29; [HRS §660-29](/title-36/chapter-660/section-660-29/)]
