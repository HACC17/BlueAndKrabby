**§660-14 For person of unknown name.** The person to be produced shall be designated by the person's name, if known, and if that is not known or is uncertain, the person may be designated in any other manner, so that it can be known who is the person intended. [L 1870, c 32, §10; RL 1925, §2737; RL 1935, §4322; RL 1945, §10363; RL 1955, §239-14; [HRS §660-14](/title-36/chapter-660/section-660-14/); gen ch 1985]
