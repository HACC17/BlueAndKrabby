**[ยง432D-20] Filings and reports as public documents.** Notwithstanding chapter 92F and any other laws to the contrary, all applications and filings required under this chapter shall be treated as public, except for trade secrets or privileged or confidential quality assurance, commercial, or financial information; provided that any annual financial statement that may be required under section 432D-5 shall be public.
