**[ยง435C-10] Examinations.** The insurance commissioner shall make an examination into the affairs of the plan at least annually. Such examination shall be conducted and the report thereon filed in the manner prescribed in section 431:2-302\. The expenses of every such examination shall be borne and paid by the plan in the manner prescribed by section 431:2-306\. [L 1975, c 161, pt of ยง1]
