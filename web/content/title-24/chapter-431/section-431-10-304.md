**[ยง431:10H-304] Disclosure of qualification for tax benefits.** (a) Every policy that is intended to be a qualified long-term care insurance contract as provided in the federal Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996, P.L. 104-191, as amended, shall be identified as such by prominently displaying and printing on page one of the policy form and the outline of coverage and in the application the following words: "This contract for long-term care insurance is intended to be a federally qualified long-term care insurance contract and may qualify you for federal and state tax benefits."

(b) Every policy that is not intended to be a qualified long-term care insurance contract as provided in the federal Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996, P.L. 104-191, as amended, shall be identified as such by prominently displaying and printing on page one of the policy form and the outline of coverage and in the application the following words: "This contract for long-term care insurance is not intended to be a federally qualified long-term care insurance contract and is not intended to qualify you for federal and state tax benefits."
