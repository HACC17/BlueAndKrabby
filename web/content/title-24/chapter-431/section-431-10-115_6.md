**ยง431:10C-115.6** **Disclosure of personal injury protection limits and payments.** Every insurer shall advise every person entitled to personal injury protection benefits, as defined in section 431:10C-103.5(a), of the maximum amount of personal injury protection benefits available under the policy within thirty days of receiving an initial notice, claim, or application for personal injury protection benefits. The disclosure of personal injury protection policy limits shall include a description of the nature of personal injury protection benefits, matters covered by personal injury protection benefits, and the procedure for submitting personal injury protection claims.
