**[ยง431:2-410]** **Deposit into the compliance resolution fund**. All moneys that have been recovered by the department of commerce and consumer affairs as a result of prosecuting insurance fraud violations pursuant to this part, including civil fines, criminal fines, administrative fines, and settlements, but not including restitution made pursuant to section 431:2-404, 431:2-405(b)(1), or 431:2-408, shall be deposited into the compliance resolution fund established pursuant to section 26-9(o).
