**§432:2-603 Annual license.** Societies that are now authorized to transact business in this State may continue to transact business until August 16, 2016\. The authority of societies and all societies hereafter issued a certificate of authority, may thereafter be extended in accordance with section 432:2-108\. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L 1988, c 363, §2(7); am L 2012, c 251, §11; am L 2015, c 63, §15]
