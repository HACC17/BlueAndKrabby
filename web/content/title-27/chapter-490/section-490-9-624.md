**ยง490:9-624 Waiver.** (a) A debtor or secondary obligor may waive the right to notification of disposition of collateral under section 490:9-611 only by an agreement to that effect entered into and authenticated after default.

(b) A debtor may waive the right to require disposition of collateral under section 490:9-620(e) only by an agreement to that effect entered into and authenticated after default.

(c) Except in a consumer-goods transaction, a debtor or secondary obligor may waive the right to redeem collateral under section 490:9-623 only by an agreement to that effect entered into and authenticated after default. [L 2000, c 241, pt of ยง1]
