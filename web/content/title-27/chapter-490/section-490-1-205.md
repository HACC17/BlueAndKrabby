**ยง490:1-205 Reasonable time; seasonableness.** (a) Whether a time for taking an action required by this chapter is reasonable depends on the nature, purpose, and circumstances of the action.

(b) An action is taken seasonably if it is taken at or within the time agreed or, if no time is agreed, at or within a reasonable time. [L 2004, c 162, pt of ยง1]
