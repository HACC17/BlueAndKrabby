**[§712A-3]** **Venue.** An action brought pursuant to this chapter may be brought in the county which the property is seized or in any county where an owner or interest-holder could be complained against for the conduct alleged to give rise to the forfeiture of the property. [L 1988, c 260, pt of §1, §7; am L 1990, c 197, §1; am L 1993, c 196, §1; am L 1996, c 104, §6]
