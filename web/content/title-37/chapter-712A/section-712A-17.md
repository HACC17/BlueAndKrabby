**[§712A-17]** **Limitation of actions.** Notwithstanding any other provision of law, forfeiture proceedings under this chapter may be commenced at any time within the period in which a criminal proceeding may be instituted for a covered offense pursuant to section 701-108\. [L 1988, c 260, pt of §1; §7; am L 1990, c 197, §1; am L 1993, c 196, §1; am L 1996, c 104, §6]
