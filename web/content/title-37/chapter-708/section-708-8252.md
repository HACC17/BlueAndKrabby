**[ยง708-8252] Arson in the second degree.** (1) A person commits the offense of arson in the second degree if the person intentionally or knowingly sets fire to or causes to be burned property and:

(a) Recklessly places another person in danger of death or bodily injury; or

(b) Knowingly or recklessly damages the property of another, without the other's consent, in an amount exceeding $1,500.

(2) Arson in the second degree is a class B felony. [L 2006, c 181, pt of ยง1]
