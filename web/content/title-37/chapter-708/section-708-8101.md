**[ยง708-8101] Making a false statement to procure issuance of a credit card.** (1) A person commits the offense of making a false statement to procure issuance of a credit card if the person makes or causes to be made, either directly or indirectly, any false statement in writing, knowing it to be false and with intent that it be relied on, respecting the person's identity or that of any other person, firm, or corporation, for the purpose of procuring the issuance of a credit card.

(2) Making a false statement to procure issuance of a credit card is a misdemeanor. [L 1986, c 314, pt of ยง61]
