**ยง707-766 Extortion in the second degree.** (1) A person commits the offense of extortion in the second degree if the person commits extortion:

(a) Of property, labor, or services the value of which exceeds $50 during any twelve-month period; or

(b) As set forth in section 707-764(2).

(2) Extortion in the second degree is a class C felony. [L 1979, c 106, pt of ยง1; am L 1993, c 28, ยง1; am L 2008, c 147, ยง6]
