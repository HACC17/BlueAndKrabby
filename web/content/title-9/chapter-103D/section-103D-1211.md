**[ยง103D-1211] Forms for annual inventory return.** The administrator of the state procurement office shall prepare and print a general form upon which the inventories required under sections 103D-1206 and 103D-1207 shall be made, and before June 1 of each year, shall mail to each officer required under sections 103D-1206 and 103D-1207 to file an annual inventory return, as many forms as necessary to enable the officer to make a proper return. The administrator of the state procurement office, at the same time, shall call to the attention of the officer, in writing, the requirements of this part. [L 1994, c 186, pt of ยง1]
