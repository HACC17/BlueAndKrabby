**§103D-209 Authority to contract for certain services.** Except as provided in section 28-8.3 with respect to agencies of the State of Hawaii, any governmental body of this State may act as a purchasing agency and contract on its own behalf for professional services subject to this chapter and rules adopted by the policy board. The purchasing agency may consult with the chief procurement officer or the officer's designee when procuring these services. [L Sp 1993, c 8, pt of §2; am L 1994, c 92, §3 and c 188, §§2, 7; am L 1995, c 178, §§6, 16; am L 1998, c 11, §6]

Note

L 1996, c 262, §25 purports to extend repeal date of June 30, 1995 to June 30, 1998.
