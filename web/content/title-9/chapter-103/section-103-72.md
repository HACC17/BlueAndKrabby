**ยง103-72 Transcription of instructional materials.** Contracts for the procurement of instructional materials shall include a provision whereby the State has the right to transcribe and reproduce the material in braille, large print, recordings, or other media for the use of physically-disabled students, including the visually impaired, unable to use the material in conventional print and form.
