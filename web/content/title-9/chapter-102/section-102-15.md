**§102-15 Violation voids contract.** After May 31, 1959, any contract awarded or executed in violation of sections 102-1 to 102-12 shall be void. [L 1959, c 245, §11; Supp, §7B-11; [HRS §102-15](/title-9/chapter-102/section-102-15/)]
