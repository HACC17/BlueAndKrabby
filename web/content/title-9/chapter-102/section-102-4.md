**§102-4 Advertisement for bids.** Public notice of a call for bids shall be made not less than three different days statewide, with respect to any state agency, or county-wide within the particular county with respect to any county or county agency. [L 1959, c 245, §3; Supp, §7B-3; [HRS §102-4](/title-9/chapter-102/section-102-4/); am L 2001, c 303, §3]
