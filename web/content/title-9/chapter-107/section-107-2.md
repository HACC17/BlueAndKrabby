**§107-2 Surveys, maps, etc., lands, harbors, etc.** The comptroller shall cause such surveys, maps, and plans of the government lands set aside for public purposes and of the harbors, and internal improvements to be made as the public interest may require, which surveys, maps, and plans shall be kept in the comptroller's office for public inspection and reference.
