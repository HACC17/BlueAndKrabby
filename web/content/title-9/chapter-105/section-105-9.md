**§105-9 Duty of performance.** The duty of carrying out sections 105-6 to 105-8, insofar as they relate to motor vehicles, the property, or under the control, of the State, shall be vested in the comptroller, and, insofar as they relate to the several counties, shall be vested in the respective county councils, and insofar as they relate to independent boards and commissions having charge of their own funds shall be vested in the respective boards or commissions. [L 1919, c 227, §3; RL 1945, §464; am L 1949, c 389, §1(c); RL 1955, §7-18; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §12; [HRS §105-9](/title-9/chapter-105/section-105-9/)]

Revision Note

"County councils" substituted for "boards of supervisors or city council".
