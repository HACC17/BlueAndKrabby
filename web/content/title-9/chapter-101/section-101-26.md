**§101-26 Final order of condemnation.** When all payments required by the final judgment have been made, the court shall make a final order of condemnation, which shall describe the property condemned and the purposes of the condemnation, a certified copy of which shall be filed and recorded in the office of the registrar of conveyances, and thereupon the property described shall vest in the plaintiff. [L 1896, c 45, §18; RL 1925, §824; RL 1935, §66; RL 1945, §317; RL 1955, §8-24; [HRS §101-26](/title-9/chapter-101/section-101-26/)]
