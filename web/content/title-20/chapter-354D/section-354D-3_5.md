**[ยง354D-3.5] Annual report.** The department shall submit to the legislature an annual report at least twenty days prior to the convening of each regular session which shall include:

(1) A listing by position number and title of all positions established under section 354D-3;

(2) The salaries paid to the employees in these positions;

(3) A description of the duties and responsibilities of each employee; and

(4) The usual work site of each employee. [L 1995, c 170, ยง3]
