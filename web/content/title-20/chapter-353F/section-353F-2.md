**[ยง353F-2] Maximum inmate populations; guidelines.** The commission shall establish for each correctional facility, maximum inmate population limits which may be enforced by the director of public safety. Population limits shall be established pursuant to guidelines adopted by the commission, which guidelines may be adopted without regard to the requirements of chapter 91\.
