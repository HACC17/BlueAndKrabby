**[§346G-2]**  **Findings and declaration of necessity.  **It is the intent of the legislature to establish a special fund within the state treasury to receive revenue from the imposition of a hospital sustainability fee to be administered by the department of humans services, which shall use the revenue from the fee and associated federal medicaid matching funds to make direct payments to hospitals and for other purposes as set forth in this chapter. [L 2012, c 217, pt of §2, §5; am L 2013, c 141, pt of §1, §2; am L 2014, c 123, §2; am L 2015, c 70, §2]
