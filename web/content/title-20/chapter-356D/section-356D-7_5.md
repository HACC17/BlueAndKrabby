**[****ยง356D-7.5] Exemption from tax on income and obligations.** Income earned and obligations issued by a nonprofit entity determined to be a "public housing agency" pursuant to section 3(b)(6) of the United States Housing Act of 1937, as amended, and which income and obligations are declared by the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development to be exempt from all taxation imposed by the United States pursuant to section 11(b) of the Act, shall be exempt from all taxation now or hereafter imposed by the State.
