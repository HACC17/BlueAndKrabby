**ยง352-21 Youth correctional facilities' benefit fund; disposition of income.** The director shall establish a youth correctional facilities' benefit fund for the purpose of the welfare and recreation of the persons committed. The director shall deposit all moneys arising from any program activities at the facilities, except those amounts credited to the committed persons' accounts pursuant to section 352**-**18, to the credit of the youth correctional facilities' benefit fund.
