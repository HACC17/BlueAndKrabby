**[****ยง353-133] Satisfaction of victims' rights to notification.** Participation in the system and making offender data available on a timely basis to the system shall be deemed to satisfy the obligations of:

(1) The department to notify the victim of changes in the offender's custodial status pursuant to section 801D-4(a)(7); and

(2) The police and prosecuting attorney to notify the victim of the offender's release from custody pursuant to section 801D-4(a)(1). [L 2012, c 190, pt of ยง1]
