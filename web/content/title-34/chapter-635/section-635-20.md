**§635-20 Less than unanimous verdict authorized.** In all civil cases tried before a jury it shall be sufficient for the return of a verdict if at least five-sixths of the jurors agree on the verdict. [L 1965, c 171, §1; Supp, §231-28; [HRS §635-20](/title-34/chapter-635/section-635-20/)]
