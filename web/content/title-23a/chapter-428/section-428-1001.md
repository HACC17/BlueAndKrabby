**PART X. FOREIGN LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES**

**[ยง428-1001] Law governing foreign limited liability companies.** (a) The laws of the state or other jurisdiction under which a foreign limited liability company is organized shall govern its organization and internal affairs and the liability of its managers, members, and their transferees.

(b) A foreign limited liability company may not be denied a certificate of authority by reason of any difference between the laws of another jurisdiction under which the foreign limited liability company is organized and the laws of this State.

(c) A certificate of authority does not authorize a foreign limited liability company to engage in any business or exercise any power that a limited liability company may not engage in or exercise in this State. [L 1996, c 92, pt of ยง1]
