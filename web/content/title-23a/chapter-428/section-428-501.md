**PART V. TRANSFEREES AND CREDITORS OF MEMBERS**

**[ยง428-501] Member's distributional interest.** (a) A member is not a co-owner of, and has no transferable interest in, property of a limited liability company.

(b) A distributional interest in a limited liability company is personal property and, subject to sections 428-502 and 428-503, may be transferred in whole or in part.

(c) An operating agreement may provide that a distributional interest may be evidenced by a certificate of the interest issued by the limited liability company and, subject to section 428-503, may also provide for the transfer of any interest represented by the certificate.
