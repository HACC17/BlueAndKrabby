**§428-109 Resignation of registered agent.** A registered agent of a domestic or foreign limited liability company may resign from the registered agent's appointment by complying with the requirements of section 425R-10\. [L 1996, c 92, pt of §1; am L 2002, c 130, §103; am L 2003, c 124, §71; am L 2009, c 55, §54]
