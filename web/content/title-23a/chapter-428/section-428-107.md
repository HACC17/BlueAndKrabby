**ยง428-107 Registered agent.** A limited liability company and a foreign limited liability company authorized to transact business in this State shall continuously maintain in this State a registered agent, who shall have a business address in this State and may be:

(1) An individual who resides in this State;

(2) A domestic entity authorized to transact business in this State; or

(3) A foreign entity authorized to transact business in this State. [L 1996, c 92, pt of ยง1; am L 2003, c 124, ยง70; am L 2009, c 55, ยง52]
