**[ยง188-58.5] Female ula (spiny lobsters), Kona crabs, and Samoan crabs; taking or killing prohibited.** (a) Unless otherwise exempted by law, it shall be unlawful for any person to take or kill any female ula (spiny lobster or Panulirus), Kona crab (Ranina ranina), or Samoan crab (Scylla serrata).

(b) This section shall not prohibit the taking or killing of female marine invertebrates identified in subsection (a) if they are the product of commercial aquaculture and not removed from their natural environment. [L 2006, c 77, ยง1]
