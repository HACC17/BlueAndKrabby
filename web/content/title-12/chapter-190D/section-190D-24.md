**[ยง190D-24] Konohiki fishing rights.** (a) The provisions of this chapter shall not abridge or alter in any way konohiki fishing rights recognized in chapter 187A, including the right to sublease private konohiki fishing grounds for marine activities. No lease shall be granted by the board which conflicts with or impairs konohiki fishing rights.

(b) Any traditional fishing or marine activity conducted within konohiki fishing grounds is subject to all applicable state laws and rules enacted pursuant to the State's police powers over fisheries and navigable waters. [L 1986, c 91, pt of ยง1]
