**[ยง174C-8] Adoption of rules concerning water resources by the commission.** The commission shall adopt and enforce such rules as may be necessary or convenient to administer this chapter. The initial set of rules, subject to later amendment, revisions, or additions, shall be adopted no later than two years after July 1, 1987\. Rules shall be adopted in conformity with chapter 91\.
