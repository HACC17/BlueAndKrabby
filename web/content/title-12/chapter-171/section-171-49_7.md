**[ยง171-49.7] Public lands suitable and available for residential development; inventory.** The department of land and natural resources shall complete and maintain a current inventory of all public lands placed in the urban district by the land use commission under chapter 205 which are or may be suitable and available for residential development. This inventory shall be updated at the end of each quarter and shall contain the following information: the island and area in which the land is situated, the acreage, and such other information which the department determines may be necessary to identify and inventory the land.
