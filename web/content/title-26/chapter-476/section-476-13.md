**§476-13 Statement and receipt of payments.** Upon written request from the buyer, the holder of a credit sale contract shall give or forward to the buyer a written statement of the dates and amounts of payments and the total amount unpaid under the contract. A buyer shall be given a written receipt for any payment when made in cash.
