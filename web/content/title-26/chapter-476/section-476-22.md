**§476-22 Cancellation of contract.** After the payment of all sums for which the buyer is obligated under a credit sale contract, the holder of the contract shall mail to the buyer at the buyer's last known address, good and sufficient instruments to indicate payment in full and to release all security for the contract, and canceled negotiable instruments, if any, evidencing the indebtedness.
