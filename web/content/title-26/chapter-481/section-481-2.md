**§481-2 Officers and agents.** Any person who, either as director, officer, or agent of any firm or corporation, or as agent of any person, violating this part, assists or aids, directly or indirectly, in such violation shall be responsible therefor equally with the person, firm, or corporation for whom or which the person acts.
