**PART III. REGISTRATION OF SECURITIES AND NOTICE**

**FILING OF FEDERAL COVERED SECURITIES**

**[ยง485A-301] Securities registration requirement.** It is unlawful for a person to offer or sell a security in this State unless:

(1) The security is a federal covered security;

(2) The security, transaction, or offer is exempted from registration under sections 485A-201 to 485A-203; or

(3) The security is registered under this chapter. [L 2006, c 229, pt of ยง1]
