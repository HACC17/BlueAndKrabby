**§480-15 Injunction by attorney general or the director of the office of consumer protection.** The attorney general may bring proceedings to enjoin any violation of this chapter; provided that the director of the office of consumer protection may also bring proceedings to enjoin any violation of section 480-2\. [L 1961, c 190, §13; Supp, §205A-13; [HRS §480-15](/title-26/chapter-480/section-480-15/); am L 1975, c 67, §1]
