**§487-4 Salary and duties of executive director; benefits.** The director may define the executive director's powers and duties and fix the executive director's compensation. The executive director and attorney staff members shall be entitled to participate in any employee benefit plan. [L 1969, c 175, §4; am L 1975, c 58, §21; am L 1982, c 129, §18; am L 1986, c 128, §16; am L 1988, c 60, §4]
