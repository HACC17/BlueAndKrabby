**ยง486-79 Citation and notice to appear.** The administrator may issue to any person violating this part, a citation and notice to appear, at a given time and place, to answer to charges against the person.

The form, contents, copy designation and disposition, and appearance dates of such citation and notice to appear shall be prescribed by the district courts. When a complaint of the violation of this part, including any rules adopted thereunder, is made to any prosecuting officer, the arresting officer or employee who issued the citation and notice to appear shall subscribe to it under oath administered by another official or officials of the department, whose name has been submitted to the prosecuting officer and who has been designated by the administrator to administer the same.
