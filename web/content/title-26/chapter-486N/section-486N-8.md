**ยง486N-8** **Duration of contracts.** (a) Every health club contract shall specify the duration of the contract. The unexpired, aggregate term of any health club contract or contracts and any extensions or renewals thereof shall at no time exceed thirty-six months per buyer. In no case shall a health club contract be measured by the life of the buyer or the life of the health club.

(b) A health club contract may not contain an automatic renewal clause. [L 1984, c 161, pt of ยง1; am L 1989, c 202, ยง3]
