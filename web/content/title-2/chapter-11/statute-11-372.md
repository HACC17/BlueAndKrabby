**[ยง****11-372] Reporting loan; written loan agreement.** (a) Every loan shall be reported as provided in section 11-333.

(b) Every loan in excess of $100 shall be documented as provided in section 11-333.

(c) A loan shall be treated as a contribution, subject to all relevant provisions of this part, if the loan is not reported or documented as provided in section 11-333\. [L 2010, c 211, pt of ยง2]
