**[ยง****11-362] Contributions limited from nonresident persons.** (a) Contributions from all persons who are not residents of the State at the time the contributions are made shall not exceed thirty per cent of the total contributions received by a candidate or candidate committee for each election period.

(b) This section shall not be applicable to contributions from the candidate's immediate family. [L 2010, c 211, pt of ยง2]
