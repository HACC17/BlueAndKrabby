**[ยง****11-339] Final election period report for candidate committee or noncandidate committee** **receiving and expending $1,000 or less during the election period.** (a) Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, a candidate committee or noncandidate committee whose aggregate contributions and aggregate expenditures for the election period total $1,000 or less, shall electronically file only a final election period report, and need not file a preliminary and final primary report, a preliminary and final general report, or a special election report.

(b) Until the candidate committee's or noncandidate committee's registration is terminated as provided in section 11-326, supplemental reports and other reports required by this part shall be filed. [L 2010, c 211, pt of ยง2]
