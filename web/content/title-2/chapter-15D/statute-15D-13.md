**[ยง15D-**<a></a><a></a><a></a><a></a><a></a><a>**13]**</a> Confirmation of receipt of application and voted ballot. The chief election officer, in coordination with local election officials, shall implement an electronic free-access system by which a covered voter may determine by telephone, electronic mail, or Internet whether:

(1) The voter's federal postcard application or other registration or military-overseas ballot application has been received and accepted; and

(2) The voter's military-overseas ballot has been received. [L 2012, c 226, pt of ยง1]
