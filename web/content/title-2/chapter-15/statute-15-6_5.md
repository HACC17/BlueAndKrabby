**[ยง15-6.5]** **Absentee postage.** The mailed distribution and return of absentee ballots shall be at no cost to the voter. The State and counties shall share in the cost of all postage associated with the distribution and return of absentee ballots pursuant to sections 11-182, 11-183, and 11-184, if the costs are not covered by the federal government.
