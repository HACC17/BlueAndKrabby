**ยง206J-20 Court proceedings; preference.** Any action or proceeding to which the development corporation, the State, or a county may be a party, in which any question arises as to the validity of this chapter, shall be preferred over all other civil causes, except election cases, without respect to position on the calendar. The same preference shall be given upon application of counsel to the development corporation in any action or proceeding questioning the validity of this chapter in which the development corporation has duly intervened.
