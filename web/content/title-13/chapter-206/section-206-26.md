**ยง206-26 Disclosure by private developer; public records.** A private developer or assign, as the term is used in this chapter, shall file with the board of land and natural resources an enumeration list of all persons directly or indirectly connected with the private developer or assign as a condition precedent to the private developer's or assign's acceptance as the private developer or assign by the board. A private developer or assign who fails to comply with this requirement shall automatically forfeit all rights to any profit under this chapter.

All bids and any or all records of a private developer or assign, relating to any and all transactions with the State, shall be public records, as defined in chapter 92, and subject to such use as permitted by chapter 92\.
