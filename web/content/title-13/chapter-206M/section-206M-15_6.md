**[ยง206M-15.6] Hawaii technology loan revolving fund.** There is established the Hawaii technology loan revolving fund for the purpose of investing in technology development in Hawaii. The following shall be deposited into the Hawaii technology loan revolving fund:

(1) Appropriations from the legislature;

(2) Moneys received as repayments of loans;

(3) Investment earnings;

(4) Royalties;

(5) Premiums, or fees or equity charged by the corporation, or otherwise received by the corporation; and

(6) Loans that are convertible to equity;

provided that the total amount of moneys in the fund shall not exceed $2,000,000 at the end of any fiscal year. [L 2000, c 297, pt of ยง22(1)]
