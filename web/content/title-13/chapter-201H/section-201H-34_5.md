**[ยง201H-34.5] Authority to modify and amend development agreements with eligible developers.** The corporation is authorized to amend, delete, restate, and otherwise modify the terms, conditions, plans, specifications, and all other matters relating, directly or indirectly, to any housing project that was previously approved by the corporation, including the terms, covenants, and conditions of any development agreement for a housing project between the corporation and an eligible developer.
