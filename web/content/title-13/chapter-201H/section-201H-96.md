**[ยง201H-96] Eligible improvement loans.** (a) The corporation shall establish requirements for property financed by an eligible improvement loan, and may consider the location, age, condition, value, and other characteristics of the property.

(b) The corporation shall establish restrictions on the terms, maturities, interest rates, collateral, and other requirements for eligible improvement loans.

(c) All eligible improvement loans made shall comply with applicable state and federal laws. [L 2006, c 180, pt of ยง4]
