**PART III. FOREIGN BANK OFFICES AND AGENCIES--REPEALED**

**§§412:5A-300 to 316 REPEALED.** L 1996, c 155, §6.nd restrictions for lending and investments by all banks. A bank may make loans and extensions of credit and may invest its assets as may be permitted by this part and as may be provided elsewhere in this article. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]
