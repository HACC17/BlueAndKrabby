**ยง412:10-412 Participation loans.** Participation loans to credit union members jointly with other credit unions, credit union service organizations, or financial organizations shall be in accordance with written policies of the board of directors. A credit union which originates a loan for which participation arrangements are made in accordance with this section shall retain an interest of at least ten per cent of the face amount of the loan.
