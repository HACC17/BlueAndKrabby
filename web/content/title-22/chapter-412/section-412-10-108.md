**ยง412:10-108 Denial of charter.** If the commissioner is not satisfied that the applicant meets all the criteria set forth for approval, the commissioner shall issue a written decision denying the applicant's application for charter. An applicant who is denied approval may request a hearing before the commissioner in accordance with chapter 91\.
