**ยง412:11-105 Sanctions for failure to register or submit reports.** Any financial institution holding company failing to register or furnish any report or information as and when required under this article, shall be subject to an administrative fine of not more than $200 for each day that it fails to register or for each day that any such report is delinquent, which fine shall be recovered pursuant to the provisions of section 412:2-611\.
