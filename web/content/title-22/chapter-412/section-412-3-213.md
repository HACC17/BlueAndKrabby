**ยง412:3-213** **Denial of charter or license.** If the commissioner is not satisfied that the applicant meets all the criteria set forth for approval of the charter or license, the commissioner shall issue a written decision denying the applicant's application. An applicant who is denied approval may request a hearing before the commissioner in accordance with chapter 91\.
