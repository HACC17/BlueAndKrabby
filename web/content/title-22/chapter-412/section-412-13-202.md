**ยง412:13-202 Application to establish and maintain a branch or agency; contents.** A foreign bank, in order to procure a license under this article to establish and maintain a Hawaii state branch or Hawaii state agency, shall submit an application to the commissioner, together with the application fee prescribed in section 412:13-206\. The application shall contain:

(1) The same information as required by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System for an application to establish a branch or agency, as the case may be, in the United States;

(2) If applicable, a statement under oath appointing an agent in this State for receipt of service of process in accordance with section 414-437, if the license is granted; and

(3) Any additional information that the commissioner may require. [L 1996, c 155, pt of ยง2; am L 2001, c 170, ยง14; am L 2002, c 40, ยง36]
