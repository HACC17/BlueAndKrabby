**[ยง143-2.6]** **Animal desertion.** It shall be unlawful for the owner of any animal or any person in possession of an animal that belongs to another person to leave the animal without the intention of returning to it.

Any person who violates this section shall be guilty of a petty misdemeanor. [L 1992, c 223, ยง1]
