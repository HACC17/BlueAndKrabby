**§88-165 Adjustments of pensions.** The board of trustees shall have full authority, within the limits set down in this part, to review all pensions previously granted or which are hereafter granted under this part and make adjustments considered fair, reasonable, or necessary. [L 1939, c 86, pt of §3; RL 1945, pt of §6173; RL 1955, §6-145; [HRS §88-165](/title-7/chapter-88/section-88-165/)]
