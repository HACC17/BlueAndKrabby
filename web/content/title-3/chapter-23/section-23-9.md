**§23-9 Reports.** The auditor shall, at each regular session, submit a report to the legislature of the audits and examinations conducted by the auditor for the immediately preceding fiscal year and all other audits and examinations conducted by the auditor during the current fiscal year, together with findings and recommendations relative to the expenditures made and financial transactions had by the departments, offices, and agencies of the State and its political subdivisions. Reports may also be submitted to the legislative council or any legislative interim committee then in existence. Certified copies of all audits and examinations made by the auditor shall be sent to the governor and the director of finance. All reports shall be available for public inspection. A certified copy of that portion of any report which consists of audits and examinations of departments, offices, or agencies of a political subdivision shall be sent to the council of the political subdivision concerned.
