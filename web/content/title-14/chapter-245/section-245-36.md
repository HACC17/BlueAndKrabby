**[ยง245-36] Counterfeiting stamps.** A person shall be guilty of a class B felony if the person:

(1) Intentionally or knowingly makes, alters, or reuses a stamp as defined in section 245-1; or

(2) Knowingly possesses or distributes a stamp that has been falsely made, altered, or reused. [L 2000, c 249, pt of ยง1]
