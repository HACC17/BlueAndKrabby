**[ยง245-30] Penalty for failure to make timely payment.** A licensee who fails to pay any amount owing for the purchase of stamps within the time required shall pay a penalty of:

(1) Ten per cent of the amount due in addition to the amount due; and

(2) Interest at the rate specified in section 231-39 from the date on which the amount became due and payable until the date of payment. [L 2000, c 249, pt of ยง1]
