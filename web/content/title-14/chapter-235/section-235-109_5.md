**[ยง235-109.5]** **Credits against income; claim limitation.** (a) Notwithstanding any law to the contrary providing for a tax credit that may be claimed against a taxpayer's tax liability under section 235-110.51, 235-110.9, 241-4.8, or 431:7-209 for taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2009, and ending before January 1, 2011, no claim for these tax credits shall exceed eighty per cent of the taxpayer's tax liability for the taxable year in which the credit is claimed, and any tax credits claimed shall not result in any credit carryovers.

(b) This section shall apply to investments made, renovation costs incurred, or eligible depreciable tangible property placed in service on or after May 1, 2009\. [L 2009, c 178, ยง1]
