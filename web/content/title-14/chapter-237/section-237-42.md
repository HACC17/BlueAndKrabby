**APPEALS**

**§237-42 Appeals.** Any person aggrieved by any assessment of the tax for any month or any year may appeal from the assessment in the manner and within the time and in all other respects as provided in the case of income tax appeals by section 235-114\. [L 1935, c 141, §1; am L 1937, c 202, §1; RL 1945, §5473; am L 1953, c 125, pt of §10; RL 1955, §117-37; am L Sp 1957, c 1, §29; [HRS §237-42](/title-14/chapter-237/section-237-42/); am L 2000, c 199, §3; am L 2004, c 123, §3]

Note

The 2004 amendment applies to tax appeals filed on or after July 1, 2004\. L 2004, c 123, §14.

Case Notes
