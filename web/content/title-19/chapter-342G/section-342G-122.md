**[ยง342G-122] Advisory committee.** The department shall convene an advisory committee to assist it in developing any rules needed to implement this chapter. The department shall select members of the committee so as to obtain input on the state level as well as assess the impact on each individual county, consumers, recyclers, and the beverage industry. Members of the committee shall be appointed by the director and shall serve at the director's pleasure. A simple majority of the committee members shall constitute a quorum for the purposes of recommending rules and providing input to the director. [L 2002, c 176, pt of ยง2]
