**ยง342G-116 Refusal of refund value payment for a deposit beverage container.** Redemption centers shall refuse to pay the refund value on any broken, corroded, or dismembered deposit beverage container, or any deposit beverage container that:

(1) Contains a free-flowing liquid;

(2) Does not properly indicate a refund value;

(3) Contains a significant amount of foreign material; or
