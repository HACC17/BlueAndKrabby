**[328D-10] Injunctive relief.** The director may institute a civil action in any court of competent jurisdiction for injunctive relief to prevent any violation of this chapter or any rule adopted to implement this chapter. The court shall have powers to grant relief in accordance with the Hawaii rules of civil procedure. [L 1988, c 304, pt of ยง1]
