**[ยง334-3.5] Employment program for the chronically mentally ill established.** There is established within the mental health division of the department of health a community-based employment program for the chronically mentally ill to (1) create business enterprises for employment of the chronically mentally ill; (2) teach the skills and attitudes which will enable the chronically mentally ill to become employed; and (3) provide support services including housing so that the chronically mentally ill can secure and maintain employment and live within the community.
