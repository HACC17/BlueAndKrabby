**ยง334-14** **Group homes for substance abusers; source of funds; disposition of receipts.** (a) All moneys to make loans for the establishment of housing in which individuals recovering from alcohol or drug abuse may reside in groups of not less than four individuals, shall be allocated by the legislature through appropriations out of the state general fund.

(b) The department shall include in its budgetary request for each upcoming fiscal period, the amounts necessary to effectuate the purposes of this section.

(c) All moneys received from other funding sources, including but not limited to federal sources, or in repayment of loan principal, payment of interest, or fees, under this section shall be deposited to the credit of the state general fund. [L 1990, c 177, ยง2; am L 1993, c 280, ยง36]
