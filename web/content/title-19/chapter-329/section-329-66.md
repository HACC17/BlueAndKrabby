**[ยง329-66] Theft,** **loss,** **and** **discrepancy** **reports.** (a) The theft or loss of any substance regulated pursuant to section 329-61 discovered by any person regulated by this part shall be reported to the department of public safety within three days of the receipt of actual knowledge of the discrepancy.

(b) Any report made pursuant to this section shall also include the name of the common carrier or person who transports the substance and date of shipment of the substance. [L 1990, c 200, pt of ยง1]
