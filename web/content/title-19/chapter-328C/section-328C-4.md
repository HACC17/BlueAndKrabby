**[ยง328C-4] Labeling of donated pharmaceuticals or health care supplies.** Any charitable, religious, or nonprofit organization which receives and distributes donated pharmaceuticals or health care supplies pursuant to this chapter shall affix a label upon those items stating that the items are donated and are not for resale and stating that they were fit for human consumption or use on the date that they left control of the charitable, religious, or nonprofit organization.
