**[ยง****323F-25]** **Regional system board; community hospitals; community health centers; collaboration.** Each regional system board and each community hospital under the jurisdiction of the corporation shall collaborate with community health centers within their respective geographic jurisdictions to maximize funding from the state and federal governments to:

(1) Maximize reimbursement for health care services provided;

(2) Acquire funds for capital investment;

(3) Provide expanded hours of service; and

(4) Ensure the provision of the appropriate level of care to the community served by each community health center. [L 2009, c 182, pt of ยง8]
