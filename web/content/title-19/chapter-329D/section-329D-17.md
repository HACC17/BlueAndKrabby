**[ยง329D-17] Prohibition of distribution of medical marijuana and medical marijuana products to minors; penalties.** (a) A person commits the offense of promoting medical marijuana or medical marijuana products to a minor if the person intentionally or knowingly distributes any amount of marijuana or manufactured marijuana products that came from a dispensary or production center to a minor who is not a registered qualifying patient.

(b) Any person who violates this section shall be guilty of a class B felony. [L 2015, c 241, pt of ยง2]
