**[ยง321-324] Powers of the department.** The department of health may:

(1) Make available to the target population quality health care services, with emphasis on preventive health services;

(2) Develop, extend, and improve the services; and

(3) Cooperate with the federal government, through its appropriate agency or instrumentality, in identifying needs, developing, extending, and improving the services, and receiving and expending all funds made available to the department by the federal government, the State, or its political subdivisions, or from any other source, including private donations, for the purposes of this part.
