**PART XXV. MATERNAL AND CHILD HEALTH PROGRAM**

**[ยง321-321] Purpose.** The purpose of this part is to enable the department of health to assure the availability of programs and services which promote the health of women of childbearing age, mothers, families, infants, children, youths, and adolescents. These programs shall further the State's goals of providing for the health and well-being of women and mothers and help to insure that healthy babies become healthy adults. [L 1988, c 203, pt of ยง1]
