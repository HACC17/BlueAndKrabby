**ยง321-472 Multidisciplinary and multiagency reviews**. The department shall conduct multidisciplinary and multiagency reviews of domestic violence fatalities, near-deaths, and suicides to reduce the incidence of preventable intimate partner homicides. The director may form domestic violence fatality review teams, as necessary, by appointing individuals to review domestic violence fatalities. A domestic violence fatality review team shall not be subject to part I, chapter 92\. [L 2006, c 82, pt of ยง1; am L 2015, c 203, ยง1]
