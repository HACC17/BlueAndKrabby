**[ยง304A-3011] Contracts with state agencies.** Any contract between the research corporation and any agency, office, department, or other administrative subdivision of the executive branch of the State shall include the following:

(1) Its termination date;

(2) Its intent and purpose;

(3) A statement establishing the full permissible extent of its applicability; and
