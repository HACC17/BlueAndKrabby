**[ยง304A-603] Capacity of minors in qualifying for state higher education loans.** Any student otherwise qualifying for a loan under the state higher education loan fund shall not be disqualified because the student is under the age of eighteen years, and for the purpose of applying for, receiving, and repaying the loan, any such person shall be deemed to have full legal capacity to act and shall have all rights, powers, privileges, and obligations of an adult with respect thereto.
