**PART II. STUDENTS**

A. Tuition and Fees

**[ยง304A-401] Tuition fees; resident, nonresident; other fees.** (a) The board of regents may charge resident and nonresident tuition fees for regular courses of instruction at any University of Hawaii campus, including any community college.

(b) The board may also charge other fees for special programs of instruction, as well as laboratory fees, course fees, fees for student activities, and an information technology user fee. The board may charge other fees for summer session or evening courses, including differential fees for nonresident students. [L 2006, c 75, pt of ยง2]
