**[ยง304A-301] State board for career and technical education; designation.** The board of regents is designated as the state board for career and technical education. The chairperson of the board of regents is designated as the chairperson of the state board for career and technical education and the president of the university is designated as the administrative officer of the state board for career and technical education.
