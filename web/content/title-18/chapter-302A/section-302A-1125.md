**ยง302A-1125 Educational objectives.** The board shall formulate policy and exercise control as may be necessary to define a common set of educational goals that the schools shall be responsible for fulfilling. The board shall also be responsible for formulating standards for measuring the efforts of each participating school toward achieving those goals each year.
