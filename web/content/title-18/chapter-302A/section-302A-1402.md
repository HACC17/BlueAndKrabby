**[ยง302A-1402] Custodian of federal funds.** The director of finance is designated as custodian of all funds received as the state apportionment under any federal appropriations for public educational purposes and the director shall disburse the funds, pursuant to the requirements, restrictions, and regulations of the federal acts under which the funds may be provided, on vouchers approved by the board, or by any subordinate thereunto duly authorized by the board.
