**[ยง302A-1802] Student conference committee.** There is created a student conference committee composed of sixteen secondary school students. There shall be two students from each of the seven local school districts, who shall be selected by the superintendent or the superintendent's designee, and two students from schools represented by the Hawaii Association of Independent Schools, who shall be selected by a representative of the Hawaii Association of Independent Schools.
