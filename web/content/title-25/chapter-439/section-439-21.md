**§439-21 Board to aid prosecution.** The board shall aid prosecuting officers in the prosecution of persons charged with violations of this chapter. [L 1949, c 397, pt of §1; RL 1955, §59-22; [HRS §439-21](/title-25/chapter-439/section-439-21/); am L 1987, c 154, §21]
