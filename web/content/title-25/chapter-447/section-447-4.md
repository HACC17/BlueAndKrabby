**§447-4 Revocation of dentist's license.** The board of dental examiners may revoke the license of any dentist who permits any dental hygienist employed by or working under the dentist's supervision to perform any dental operation other than that permitted under this chapter. [L 1931, c 158, §2; RL 1935, §1003; RL 1945, §2184; RL 1955, §62-4; [HRS §447-4](/title-25/chapter-447/section-447-4/); gen ch 1985; am L 1992, c 202, §70]
