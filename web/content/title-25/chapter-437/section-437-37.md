**§437-37 Credit sale contract violations; penalty.** Whoever violates any of the provisions of sections 437-32 to 437-34 relating to sales or purchase of credit sale contracts shall be fined not less than $25 nor more than $1,000\. [L 1939, c 258, §20; RL 1945, §7381; am L 1951, c 90, pt of §1; RL 1955, §160-182; [HRS §437-37](/title-25/chapter-437/section-437-37/); am L 1987, c 283, §30]
