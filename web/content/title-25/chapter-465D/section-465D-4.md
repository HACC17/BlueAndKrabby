**[ยง465D-4] License required.** (a) Beginning on January 1, 2016, except as specifically provided in this chapter, no person shall engage in the practice of behavior analysis or use the title "licensed behavior analyst" or "behavior analyst" without a valid license issued pursuant to this chapter.

(b) Any person who violates this section shall be subject to a fine of not more than $1,000 for each separate offense. Each day of each violation shall constitute a separate offense. [L 2015, c 199, pt of ยง2]
