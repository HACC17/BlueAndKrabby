**§454M-10** **Penalty.** Any person who violates any provision of this chapter may be subject to an administrative fine of not more than $7,000 for each violation; provided that if the aggregate fine amount exceeds $7,000, $1,000 of the aggregate fine amount shall be deposited into the mortgage foreclosure dispute resolution special fund established pursuant to section 667-86\. [L 2009, c 106, pt of §1; am L 2011, c 48, §11; am L 2012, c 182, §5; am L 2016, c 122, §26]
