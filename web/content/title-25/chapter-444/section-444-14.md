**§444-14 Place of business and posting of license.** (a) A licensed contractor shall have, maintain, and operate from a definite place of business in the State and shall display therein his or her contractor's license.

(b) The licensed contractor shall report any change of address or telephone number to the contractors license board within ten business days from such change. [L 1957, c 305, §1(s 13); Supp, §166A-13; [HRS §444-14](/title-25/chapter-444/section-444-14/); am L 1975, c 129, §1]
