Note

Sections 445-1 to 445-15 designated and amended as Part I by L 1990, c 164, §28.

Case Notes

Cited: 38 H. 433, 434 (1949).

**§445-1 Definition: "Treasurer".** Whenever used in this chapter "treasurer" means the treasurer or director of finance of any county of the State.
