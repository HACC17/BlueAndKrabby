**[ยง461J-9] Temporary license.** The board shall establish rules for issuing temporary licenses to applicants for permanent licenses either under section 461J-6 or 461J-7\. The rules adopted by the board shall include provisions concerning the length of time for which temporary licenses are valid, the renewal of such licenses, and the need for supervision by a physical therapist holding a permanent license.
