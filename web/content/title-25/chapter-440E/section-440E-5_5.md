**[****§440E-5.5]  Control and supervision of amateur mixed martial arts contests.** Amateur mixed martial arts contests may be placed under the control and supervision of any recognized national amateur mixed martial arts association. The director shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 91 for the purposes of this section. [L 2010, c 117, pt of §1]
