**[§457-9.5] Center for nursing fee.** [(a)] Upon the issuance of a new license and at each license renewal period, each nurse shall pay an additional fee of $40, which shall be deposited in a separate account in the compliance resolution fund established pursuant to section 26-9(o).

[(b)] The director of commerce and consumer affairs shall disburse on a quarterly basis from the compliance resolution fund, established pursuant to section 26-9(o), to the credit of the center for nursing special fund all moneys deposited in the separate account established pursuant to [this section]. [L 2003, c 198, §§3, 6, 10; am L 2008, c 173, §2; am L 2012, c 53, §1]

Revision Note
