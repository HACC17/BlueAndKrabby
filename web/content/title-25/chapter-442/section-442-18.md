**§442-18 Disposition of fees; establishment of fees by rule.** (a) All moneys received by the board under this chapter shall be paid to the director of commerce and consumer affairs and shall be deposited to the credit of the compliance resolution fund established pursuant to section 26-9(o).

(b) All fees required by this chapter or in rules adopted by the board shall be as provided in rules adopted by the director of commerce and consumer affairs pursuant to chapter 91\. The examination fee may be paid directly to the testing agency by the director or the examinee.
