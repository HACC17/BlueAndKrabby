**§459-13 Exceptions.** Nothing in this chapter shall apply to duly licensed physicians authorized to practice medicine under the laws of the State, nor to the commissioned officers of the United States Army Medical Corps on duty in the State. [L 1917, c 187, §12; RL 1925, §1123; RL 1935, §1381; RL 1945, §2812; RL 1955, §68-13; [HRS §459-13](/title-25/chapter-459/section-459-13/)]
