**§440-20 Judges; duties.** The boxing commission may in its discretion appoint two judges to act with the referee in rendering a decision or three judges to act with a nonvoting referee in rendering a decision. [L 1929, c 216, §23; RL 1935, §7010; RL 1945, §7571; am L 1949, c 264, §11; RL 1955, §165-22; [HRS §440-20](/title-25/chapter-440/section-440-20/); am L 1983, c 241, §17]
