**§440-23 Sham boxing contest; penalty against contestant.** Any contestant who knowingly participates in any sham or false boxing contest shall be suspended by the boxing commission for not less than twelve months from the date of the offense from further participation in any boxing contest held or given under this chapter and may be permanently disqualified from further participation in any boxing contest held or given under this chapter.
