**§440-30 Control and supervision of amateur boxing contest.** Amateur boxing contests may be placed by the boxing commissioner under the control and supervision of any recognized national amateur athletic association whose standing has first been approved by the commission, subject, however, to the rules as may be prescribed by the commission. [L 1929, c 216, §33; RL 1935, §7020; RL 1945, §7581; RL 1955, §165-32; [HRS §440-30](/title-25/chapter-440/section-440-30/); am L 1983, c 241, §23]
