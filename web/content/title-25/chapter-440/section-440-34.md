**§440-34 Violations; penalty.** Any individual or club in violation of this chapter or the rules of the commission shall be fined not more than $5,000 for each violation or imprisoned not more than one year, or both. [L 1929, c 216, §39; RL 1935, §7024; RL 1945, §7585; RL 1955, §165-36; [HRS §440-34](/title-25/chapter-440/section-440-34/); am L 1976, c 15, §3; am L 2004, c 135, §22]
