**§440-33 Disposition of receipts.** All moneys received by the boxing commission shall be deposited by the director of commerce and consumer affairs with the director of finance to the credit of the general fund of the State. [L 1929, c 216, §36; am L 1933, c 39, §1; RL 1935, §7023; RL 1945, §7584; am L 1953, c 165, §3; RL 1955, §165-35; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §14; am L 1963, c 114, §1; [HRS §440-33](/title-25/chapter-440/section-440-33/); am L 1983, c 241, §26]
