**§465-2 License required.** Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, it shall be unlawful to represent one's self as a psychologist or engage in the practice of psychology without having first obtained a license as provided in this chapter. [L 1967, c 290, pt of §1; [HRS §465-2](/title-25/chapter-465/section-465-2/); am L 1971, c 84, §2; am L 1985, c 115, §4]
