**§438-8 Requisites for admission to examinations.** (a) The executive secretary of the board shall determine the sufficiency of the preliminary qualifications of barber applicants for admission to examinations.

(b) The board shall contract with a professional testing service to have the testing service prepare and provide examinations for applicants as may be required for the purposes of this chapter. The examinations shall not be confined to any specific system or method, and the examinations shall be consistent with the practical and theoretical requirements as provided by this chapter.

(c) Every applicant who is required by the board to be examined shall pay an examination fee as provided in rules adopted by the director pursuant to chapter 91\.
