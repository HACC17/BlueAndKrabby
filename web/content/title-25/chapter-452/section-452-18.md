**§452-18 Sanitary rule.** The department of health shall prescribe such sanitary rules as it deems necessary, with particular reference to the precautions necessary to be employed to prevent the creating and spreading of infectious and contagious diseases. [L 1947, c 192, §18; RL 1955, §63-18; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; [HRS §452-18](/title-25/chapter-452/section-452-18/)]
