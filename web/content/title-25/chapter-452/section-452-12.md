**§452-12 Application for examination.** Each person who desires to practice the occupation of massage therapist shall file with the board a written application on a form prescribed and supplied by the board, and shall submit such credentials as may be required by this chapter or the rules adopted by the board, and shall also pay the board the required fees. [L 1947, c 192, §12; RL 1955, §63-12; [HRS §452-12](/title-25/chapter-452/section-452-12/); am L 1980, c 208, §14; am L 1997, c 40, §11]
