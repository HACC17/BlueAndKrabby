**§469-5 Disposition of fees.** All fees collected by the board shall be deposited by the department of health with the director of finance to the credit of the general fund. [L 1933, c 36, §4; RL 1935, §1453; RL 1945, §3055; RL 1955, §72-5; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §§14, 19; am L 1961, c 184, §16; am L 1963, c 114, §1; [HRS §469-5](/title-25/chapter-469/section-469-5/)]
