**§457B-3.1 Conditions concerning qualifications for licensure examination.** The director shall adopt rules setting minimum educational, training, and experience qualifications that must be satisfied before an applicant is allowed to sit for the licensing examination. [L 1985, c 199, §1; am L 1989, c 103, §2; am L 1994, c 184, §4; am L 1997, c 235, §4]
