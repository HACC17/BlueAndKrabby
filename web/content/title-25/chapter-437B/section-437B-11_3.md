**§437B-11.3 Aiding or abetting.** Aiding or abetting an unlicensed person to evade this chapter or knowingly combining or conspiring with an unlicensed person, or allowing one's license to be used by an unlicensed person, or acting as agent or partner or associate, or otherwise, of an unlicensed person with the intent to evade this chapter, shall be a misdemeanor. [L 1986, c 150, §1; am L 1998, c 199, §§8, 10]
