**[ยง436M-6] Audible alarm systems.** Every audible alarm system shall have a device to automatically terminate the audible signal within fifteen minutes of activation. No audible alarm system, whether in use prior to or after November 8, 1986, shall be used, sold, or leased unless the system complies with this section. [L 1986, c 134, pt of ยง1]
