**§441-30 Form of licenses.** The form of every license shall be prescribed by the director, and shall be issued in the name of the department, and signed by the director. [L 1967, c 199, pt of §1; [HRS §441-30](/title-25/chapter-441/section-441-30/); am L 1975, c 21, §14; am L 1981, c 221, §16]
