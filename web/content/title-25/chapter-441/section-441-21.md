**§441-21 No cemetery or pre-need funeral authority license issued when.** No cemetery or pre-need funeral authority license shall be issued:

(1) To any person unless the person has filed an application therefor;

(2) To any person who does not possess financial integrity;

(3) To any person unless it is a religious institution, corporation, county, or any association which has a perpetual existence;

(4) To any person unless the person files with the director a bond as required by section 441-22;

(5) To any person failing to establish and maintain pre-need trusts and perpetual care funds as required by this chapter;

(6) To any person failing to file with the director a copy of the documentation as required by either section 441-37(a) and (b) or section 441-37(a) and (c);

(7) To any person failing to file with the director a copy of contract form as required by section 441-32.5\. [L 1967, c 199, pt of §1; [HRS §441-21](/title-25/chapter-441/section-441-21/); am L 1969, c 242, §4; am L 1975, c 21, §6; am L 1981, c 221, §6; am L 1982, c 66, §3; am L 1985, c 101, §3]
