**§449-5.5 Net capital.** The net capital of any corporation engaging in the escrow depository business under this chapter shall not be less than $100,000\. The amount of the minimum net capital of $100,000 shall be maintained at all times by the licensee. [L 1973, c 174, §§1, 2; am L 1976, c 101, §1; am L 2013, c 166, §4]
