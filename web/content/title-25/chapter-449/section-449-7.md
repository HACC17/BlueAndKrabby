**§449-7 Investigation and ruling.** The commissioner shall make an investigation into the information furnished by the applicant and may require the applicant to furnish additional information. If the commissioner is satisfied, with or without a hearing upon the application, that the applicant has met all the criteria set forth for approval, the commissioner shall approve the application.
