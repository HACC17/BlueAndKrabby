**§454F-2.5 Exempt sponsoring mortgage loan originator company; registration.**  Any person exempt from the licensing provisions of this chapter may register with NMLS for the purpose of sponsoring a mortgage loan originator required to be licensed by this chapter. [L 2011, c 194, pt of §1; am L 2013, c 168, §20]
