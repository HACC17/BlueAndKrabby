**[ยง58-2] County arborist advisory committees; establishment.** Each county of the State shall establish a county arborist advisory committee, which shall be appointed by the mayor and shall include the county planning director, or the director's designee; one member who shall be actively employed in the practice of landscape architecture, and not less than three other members selected on the basis of active participation in programs of community beautification, or research or organization in the ecological sciences, including ethnobotany, or Hawaiiana.
