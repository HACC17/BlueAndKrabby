**§50-4 Qualifications of commission members.** Each charter commission member shall be a registered voter and resident of the county for at least three years prior to the member's appointment. Elected officials of the state or county governments shall not be eligible for appointment. [L 1963, c 73, pt of §2; Supp, §143A-4; [HRS §50-4](/title-6/chapter-50/section-50-4/); gen ch 1985]

Attorney General Opinions

Under §78-4 member of board or commission is not eligible for appointment to charter commission. Att. Gen. Op. 63-29.
