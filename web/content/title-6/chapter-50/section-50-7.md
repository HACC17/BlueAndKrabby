**ยง50-7 Powers of the commission.** The charter commission shall hold public hearings and sponsor public forums and in general provide for the widest possible public information and discussion respecting the purpose and progress of its work. It shall receive the assistance of any officer or employee of the county without extra compensation as it may request to carry out its functions. It may, with the approval of the legislative body of the county, appoint staff members and consultants as it deems necessary.
