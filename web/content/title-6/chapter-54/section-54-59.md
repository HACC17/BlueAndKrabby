**§54-59 Audits.** The auditor of the county shall make an annual audit of the accounts and financial status of the board of water supply and shall file a copy of the audit report with the council of the county. The board may, whenever necessary in connection with the issuance of any bonds, employ a certified public accountant to make audits.
