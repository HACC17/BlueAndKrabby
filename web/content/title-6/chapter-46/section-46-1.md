**§46-1 Meeting place of council.** All meetings, regular or special, of the council may be held at such places within the county other than the county seat as the council shall designate. [L 1963, c 19, §1; Supp, §138-50; [HRS §46-1](/title-6/chapter-46/section-46-1/)]
