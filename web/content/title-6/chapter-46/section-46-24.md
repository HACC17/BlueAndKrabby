**§46-24 Limitation on salary of first deputy or assistant.** Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, the salary of any first deputy or first assistant to the head of any department of the county governments shall not exceed a sum equal to ninety-five per cent of the salary of such department head. [L 1957, c 170, §4; am L 1959, c 255, §10a; am imp L 1965, c 223, pt of §5; Supp, §5-20; [HRS §46-24](/title-6/chapter-46/section-46-24/)]
