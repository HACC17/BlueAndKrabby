**§48-6 Annual report.** The department of business, economic development, and tourism shall make an annual report to the governor and the legislature, including recommendations to further the purposes of this chapter. [L 1964, c 58, pt of §2; Supp, §140A-7; [HRS §48-6](/title-6/chapter-48/section-48-6/); am L 1987, c 336, §7; am L 1990, c 293, §8]
