**ยง47-44 Issuance of transferable certificate.** If a lost, stolen, destroyed, wholly or in part, or defaced bond, coupon or both, as the case may be, will mature, will be called for redemption or will become due, as the case may be, within a period of one year from the date of request for replacement, the director of finance may issue to the claimant a transferable certificate for the face value of the bond, coupon or both, as the case may be, such certificate to be in such form as shall be prescribed by the director of finance.
