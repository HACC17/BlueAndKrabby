**§27-2 Assignment of functions by governor; report.** The governor may assign the functions in section 27-1, except the administration and operation of district courts, to such respective department or departments as can most appropriately and effectively perform the functions. [L 1965, c 97, §2; Supp, §14B-2; [HRS §27-2](/title-4/chapter-27/section-27-2/)]

Cross References

Transfer of district courts to the judiciary branch, see L 1965, c 97, §21.
