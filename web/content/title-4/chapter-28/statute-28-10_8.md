**[ยง28-10.8] Rules.** The department of the attorney general may adopt, amend, or repeal rules, pursuant to chapter 91, that may be necessary or convenient for the performance of its functions. The department's rules may include general rules of practice and procedure that apply to all administrative offices, boards, and commissions placed or established within the department without the necessity of individual adoption by the administrative offices, boards, or commissions within the department.
