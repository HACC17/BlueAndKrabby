**ARTICLE 4\. GENERAL PARTNERS**

**[ยง425E-401] General partner.** A person becomes a general partner:

(1) As provided in the partnership agreement;

(2) Under section 425E-801(3)(B) following the dissociation of a limited partnership's last general partner;

(3) As the result of a conversion or merger under article 11; or

(4) With the consent of all the partners. [L 2003, c 210, pt of ยง1]
