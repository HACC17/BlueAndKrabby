**[ยง425E-**506] Distribution in kind.**** A partner shall not have a right to demand or receive any distribution from a limited partnership in any form other than cash. Subject to section 425E-812(b), a limited partnership may distribute an asset in kind to the extent each partner receives a percentage of the asset equal to the partner's share of distributions.
