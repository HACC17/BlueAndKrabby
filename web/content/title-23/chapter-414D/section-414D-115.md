**§414D-115 Other methods of electing directors.** A corporation may provide in its articles or bylaws for the election of directors by members or delegates:

(1) On the basis of chapter or other organizational unit;

(2) By region or other geographic unit;

(3) By preferential voting; or

(4) By any other reasonable method. [L 2001, c 105, pt of §1; am L 2002, c 130, §51; am L 2003, c 87, §1; am L 2011, c 37, §9]
