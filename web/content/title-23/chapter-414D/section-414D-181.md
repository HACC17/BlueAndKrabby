**PART IX. AMENDMENT OF ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION AND BYLAWS**

**[ยง414D-181] Authority to amend.** A corporation may amend its articles of incorporation at any time to add or change a provision that is required or permitted in the articles or to delete a provision not required in the articles. Whether a provision is required or permitted in the articles is determined as of the effective date of the amendment. [L 2001, c 105, pt of ยง1]
