**PART V. OFFICE AND REGISTERED AGENT**

**§414D-71 Registered agent.** Each corporation shall continuously maintain in this State a registered agent, who shall have a business address in this State and shall be:

(1) An individual who resides in this State;

(2) A domestic entity authorized to transact business or conduct affairs in this State; or

(3) A foreign entity authorized to transact business or conduct affairs in this State. [L 2001, c 105, pt of §1; am L 2002, c 130, §40; am L 2003, c 124, §27; am L 2012, c 58, §5]
