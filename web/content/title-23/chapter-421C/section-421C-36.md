**§421C-36 Exemption of voting stock from registration.** Membership stock or membership certificates under section 421C-3(a), or share or membership capital of any association organized under or existing prior to passage of this chapter shall be included as exempt securities under section 485A-201\. [L 1982, c 97, pt of §2; am L 2006, c 229, §13]

Note

Effective date of L 1982, c 97 is May 15, 1982.
