**[ยง414-20] Penalty for signing false document.** (a) A person commits an offense if the person signs a document the person knows is false in any material respect with intent that the document be delivered to the department director for filing.

(b) An offense under this section is a class C felony. [L 2000, c 244, pt of ยง1]
