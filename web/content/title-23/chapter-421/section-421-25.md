**§421-25 General corporation laws, when applicable.** Except where otherwise provided, the general corporation laws shall apply to cooperative associations organized under this chapter. [L 1949, c 234, pt of §1; am L 1953, c 137, §1(e); RL 1955, §176-27; [HRS §421-25](/title-23/chapter-421/section-421-25/); am L 1983, c 167, §17; am L 1985, c 270, §4]
