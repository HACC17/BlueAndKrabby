**§**425**-153 Statement of qualification.** A statement of qualification shall contain:

(1) The name of the partnership;

(2) A statement that the partnership elects to be a limited liability partnership; and

(3) The mailing address of the partnership's initial principal office and the information required by section 425R-4(a). [L 2000, c 218, pt of §1; am L 2001, c 129, §73; am L 2002, c 130, §86; am L 2004, c 121, §30; am L 2009, c 55, §35]
