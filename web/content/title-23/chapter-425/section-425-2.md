**§425-2 Forms to be furnished by director.** The registration, annual and other statements required by this part shall be filed on forms to be furnished by the director of commerce and consumer affairs. [L 1969, c 247, pt of §1; am L 1980, c 270, §2; am L 1982, c 204, §8; am L 1983, c 124, §17]
