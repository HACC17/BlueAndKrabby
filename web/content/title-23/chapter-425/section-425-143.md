**ยง425-143 Partner's liability to other partners after dissolution.** (a) Except as otherwise provided in subsection (b) and section 425-117, after dissolution a partner is liable to the other partners for the partner's share of any partnership liability incurred under section 425-141.

(b) A partner who, with knowledge of the dissolution, incurs a partnership liability under section 425-141(2) by an act that is not appropriate for winding up the partnership business is liable to the partnership for any damage caused to the partnership arising from the liability.
