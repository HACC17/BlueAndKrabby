**[ยง124A-142] Military property; loss, damage, destruction, or wrongful disposition.** Any person subject to this chapter who, without proper authority:

(1) Sells or otherwise disposes of;

(2) Wilfully or through neglect damages, destroys, or loses; or

(3) Wilfully or through neglect suffers to be damaged, destroyed, sold, or wrongfully disposed of;

any military property of the United States or of the State shall be punished as a court-martial may direct. [L 1982, c 171, pt of ยง2]
