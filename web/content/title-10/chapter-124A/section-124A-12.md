**[ยง124A-12] Apprehension of deserters.** Any civil officer having authority to apprehend offenders under the laws of the United States or this State or of a state, territory, commonwealth, or possession, or the District of Columbia may summarily apprehend a deserter from the state military forces and deliver the deserter into the custody of the state military forces. If an offender is apprehended outside the State, the offender's return to the area shall be in accordance with normal extradition procedures or reciprocal agreement.
