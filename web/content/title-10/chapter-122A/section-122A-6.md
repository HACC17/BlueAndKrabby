**ยง122A-6 Discipline.** (a) The discipline of the Hawaii state defense force shall, to the extent practicable, conform to that of the Hawaii army national guard.

(b) When in the active service of the Hawaii state defense force, members of the Hawaii state defense force are subject to chapter 124A. Members are deemed to be in the active service of the Hawaii state defense force from the date and time specified in any order lawfully calling them into such service.
