**§132-12 Court aid.** Each county fire chief may invoke the aid of any court of competent jurisdiction to enforce any order or action made or taken by the fire chief in pursuance of law. [L 1917, c 115, pt of §49; RL 1925, pt of §3463; RL 1935, pt of §6839; am L 1939, c 239, §1(20); RL 1945, §8537; RL 1955, §184-14; am L 1965, c 96, §127; [HRS §132-14](/title-10/chapter-132/section-132-14/); am and ren L 1978, c 241, §2(15); gen ch 1985; am L 1993, c 6, §7]
