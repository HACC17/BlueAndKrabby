**§834-2 Definition.** The phrase "appropriate court" as used in the Agreement on Detainers shall, with reference to the courts of this State, mean the supreme court and the circuit courts. [L 1965, c 160, §2; Supp, §250A-2; [HRS §714-2](/title-37/chapter-714/section-714-2/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
