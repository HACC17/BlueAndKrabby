**§834-6 Administrator.** The lieutenant governor shall be the officer who shall serve as central administrator and information agent for the Agreement on Detainers. [L 1965, c 160, §6; Supp, §250A-6; [HRS §714-6](/title-37/chapter-714/section-714-6/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
