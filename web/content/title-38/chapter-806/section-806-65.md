**§806-65 Depositions, right to inspect.** All persons under trial shall be entitled at the time of their trial to inspect without fee or reward all depositions, or copies of the depositions, which have been taken against them, and delivered in manner by law required to the proper officer of the court before which the trial is had. [L 1876, c 40, §63; RL 1925, §4076; RL 1935, §5530; RL 1945, §10836; RL 1955, §258-46; [HRS §711-67](/title-37/chapter-711/section-711-67/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
