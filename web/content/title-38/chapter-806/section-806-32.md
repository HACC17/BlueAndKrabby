**§806-32 Repugnancy.** No indictment is invalid by reason of any repugnant allegation contained therein, provided that an offense is indicated under section 806-34\. [L 1915, c 215, pt of §2; RL 1925, §4045; RL 1935, §5504; RL 1945, §10806; RL 1955, §258-16; [HRS §711-32](/title-37/chapter-711/section-711-32/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
