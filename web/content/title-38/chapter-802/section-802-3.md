**§802-3 Request for appointment of counsel.** Any person entitled to representation by a public defender or other appointed counsel may at any reasonable time request any judge to appoint counsel to represent the person. [L 1971, c 185, pt of §1; [HRS §705C-3](/title-37/chapter-705C/section-705C-3/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1 and am L 1972, c 44, §1(b); gen ch 1985]
