**§802-8 Office of the state public defender.** An office of state public defender is hereby created within the department of budget and finance for administrative purposes. [L 1971, c 185, pt of §1; [HRS §705C-8](/title-37/chapter-705C/section-705C-8/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; am L 1980, c 302, pt of §2]
