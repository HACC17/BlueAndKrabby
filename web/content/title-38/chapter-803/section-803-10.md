**§803-10 Penalty.** Any person violating or failing to comply with section 803-9 shall be fined not more than $500 or imprisoned not more than one year, or both. [L 1927, c 261, §2; RL 1935, §5409; RL 1945, §10710; RL 1955, §255-10; [HRS §708-10](/title-37/chapter-708/section-708-10/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
