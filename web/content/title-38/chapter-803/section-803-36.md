**§803-36 Notice to applicant.** Before executing the warrant the officer shall give notice to the person who applied for it, that the person may be present and identify the property if found. [PC 1869, c 48, §7; RL 1925, §3964; RL 1935, §5416; RL 1945, §10721; RL 1955, §255-21; [HRS §708-36](/title-37/chapter-708/section-708-36/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

Case Notes

Must be given before service. 3 H. 393 (1872).
