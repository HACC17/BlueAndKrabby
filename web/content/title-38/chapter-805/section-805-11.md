**§805-11 Same; trial.** At the appointed time in the summons the district judge shall proceed to hear and try the case as in the case of a natural person. [L 1893-4, c 24, §3; RL 1925, §4015; RL 1935, §5478; RL 1945, §10778; RL 1955, §257-11; [HRS §710-11](/title-37/chapter-710/section-710-11/); am L 1970, c 188, §39; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
