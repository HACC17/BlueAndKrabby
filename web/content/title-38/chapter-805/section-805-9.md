**§805-9 Procedure against corporations; summons, issuance.** Upon an information or presentment against a corporation, the district judge shall issue a summons signed by the district judge, with the district judge's name of office, requiring the corporation to appear before the district judge at a specified time and place, to answer the charge. [L 1893-4, c 24, §1; RL 1925, §4013; RL 1935, §5476; RL 1945, §10776; RL 1955, §257-9; [HRS §710-9](/title-37/chapter-710/section-710-9/); am L 1970, c 188, §39; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

Obtaining the appearance of defendant, see HRPP rule 9.
