**§804-7 Release after bail.** When bail is offered and taken the prisoner shall be discharged from custody or imprisonment. [PC 1869, c 50, §4; RL 1925, §3982; RL 1935, §5436; RL 1945, §10737; RL 1955, §256-7; [HRS §709-7](/title-37/chapter-709/section-709-7/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
