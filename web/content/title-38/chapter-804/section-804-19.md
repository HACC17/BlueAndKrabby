**§804-19 Times for appearance.** Witnesses bound to appear, and persons let out on bail, shall not only attend on the day appointed in their respective obligations, but at such other times as the court shall direct, and the obligation continues until they are discharged by the court. [PC 1869, c 50, §18; RL 1925, §3995; RL 1935, §5449; RL 1945, §10749; RL 1955, §256-19; [HRS §709-19](/title-37/chapter-709/section-709-19/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
