**PART I. BAIL; RECOGNIZANCE**

Law Journals and Reviews

Risky Business: Assessing Dangerousness in Hawaii. 24 UH L. Rev. 63 (2001).

**§804-1 Bail defined.** Bail, or the giving of bail, is the signing of the recognizance by the defendant and the defendant's surety or sureties, conditioned for the appearance of the defendant at the session of a court of competent jurisdiction to be named in the condition, and to abide by the judgment of the court.
