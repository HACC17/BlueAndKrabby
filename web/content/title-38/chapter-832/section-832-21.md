**§832-21 Governor may recall warrant or issue alias.** The governor may recall the governor's warrant of arrest or may issue another warrant whenever the governor deems proper. [L 1941, c 99, §21; RL 1945, §10651; RL 1955, §250-21; [HRS §713-21](/title-37/chapter-713/section-713-21/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]
