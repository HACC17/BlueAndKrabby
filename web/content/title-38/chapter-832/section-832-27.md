**§832-27 No right of asylum; no immunity from other criminal prosecutions while in this State.** After a person has been brought back to this State by or after waiver of extradition proceedings, the person may be tried in this State for other crimes which the person may be charged with having committed here as well as that specified in the requisition for the person's extradition. [L 1941, c 99, §27; RL 1945, §10657; RL 1955, §250-27; [HRS §713-27](/title-37/chapter-713/section-713-27/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]
