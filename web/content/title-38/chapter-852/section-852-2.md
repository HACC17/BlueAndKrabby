**§852-2 Penalty.** Any person who refuses or wilfully fails to move as directed by such officer shall be fined not more than $200 or imprisoned not more than six months, or both. [L Sp 1949, c 9, §2; RL 1955, §297-2; [HRS §754-2](/title-37/chapter-754/section-754-2/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
