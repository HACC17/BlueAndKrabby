**§831-1 Definition.** In this chapter, "felony" means an offense that is punishable with imprisonment for a term which is in excess of one year. [L 1969, c 250, pt of §1; [HRS §716-1](/title-37/chapter-716/section-716-1/); ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; am L 1975, c 14, §1]
